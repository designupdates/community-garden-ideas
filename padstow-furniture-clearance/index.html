Tips

To help you prepare for a successful garden furniture clearance, here are some practical tips to follow:

Tip 1: Sort and Categorize:

Before the clearance team arrives, take some time to sort and categorize your garden furniture. Separate items you want to keep, donate, recycle, or dispose of. This will make the clearance process more efficient and organized.

Tip 2: Disassemble Furniture:

If possible, disassemble your garden furniture into smaller, more manageable pieces. This will make it easier for the clearance team to carry and load the furniture onto their vehicles. It will also save space during transportation.

Tip 3: Prepare Your Garden Space:

Clear a path to your garden furniture and remove any obstacles that may hinder the clearance team's access. Trim overgrown plants and branches to create a safe and accessible workspace.

Tip 4: Be Present During Clearance:

It's advisable to be present during the garden furniture clearance to supervise the process and answer any questions the clearance team may have. This will ensure that the clearance is carried out according to your preferences and that any special instructions are followed.
