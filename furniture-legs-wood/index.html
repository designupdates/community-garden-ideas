Here are a few tips for repairing wood furniture legs:

  • If the leg is cracked, you can use wood glue to repair it. Simply apply the glue to the crack and clamp the leg together until the glue dries.
  • If the leg is broken, you can use wood filler to repair it. Simply apply the wood filler to the break and smooth it out with a putty knife. Once the wood filler dries, you can sand it and paint it to match the finish of the leg.
  • If the leg is loose, you can use a nail to tighten it. Simply drive a nail through the leg and into the furniture frame. Be sure to countersink the nail so that it is flush with the surface of the leg.
