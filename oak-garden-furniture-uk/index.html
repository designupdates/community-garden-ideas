Natural beauty: Showcases the unique grain patterns of oak.

Oak is renowned for its beautiful and unique grain patterns, which add character and warmth to any outdoor space. Each piece of oak garden furniture UK is a one-of-a-kind work of art, with its own distinctive grain patterns and color variations.

  • Straight grain: Straight grain oak has a uniform, linear grain pattern that runs parallel to the length of the wood. This type of grain pattern is often found in younger oak trees and is known for its clean and classic look.
  • Quartersawn grain: Quartersawn oak is cut from the log in a way that reveals the medullary rays of the wood. This results in a distinctive grain pattern that resembles rays or flakes. Quartersawn oak is known for its strength, stability, and beautiful visual appeal.
  • Rift-sawn grain: Rift-sawn oak is cut from the log in a way that minimizes the appearance of medullary rays. This results in a straight, even grain pattern that is known for its durability and resistance to warping.
  • Burr oak: Burr oak is a rare and highly prized type of oak that is known for its unique and beautiful grain patterns. Burr oak grows in clusters, and the wood from these clusters has a distinctive burl pattern that is highly sought after by furniture makers.

The natural beauty of oak garden furniture UK makes it a timeless and elegant choice for any outdoor space. Whether you prefer the classic look of straight grain oak or the more distinctive patterns of quartersawn or rift-sawn oak, you are sure to find a piece of furniture that complements your style and adds a touch of natural beauty to your garden.
