  • Acacia: Acacia is another durable hardwood that is similar to teak. It is slightly less expensive than teak, but it is still a very good choice for garden furniture.
  • Eucalyptus: Eucalyptus is a fast-growing hardwood that is also very durable. It is a good choice for those who are on a budget.
  • Wicker: Wicker is a natural material that is made from the stems of rattan plants. It is a very lightweight and flexible material, so it is easy to move around. However, it is not as durable as other materials, so it is important to protect it from the elements.
  • Aluminum: Aluminum is a lightweight and durable metal that is resistant to rust and corrosion. It is a good choice for those who live in areas with harsh weather conditions.
