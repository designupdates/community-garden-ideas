Here is a simple recipe for lavender furniture polish:

  1. 1/2 cup beeswax
  2. 1/4 cup lavender essential oil
  3. 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Melt the beeswax in a double boiler.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in the lavender essential oil and olive oil.
  3. Pour the mixture into a jar or container and allow it to cool.

To use the furniture polish, simply apply a small amount to a clean cloth and rub it into the wood surface. Buff with a clean cloth to achieve a beautiful shine.
