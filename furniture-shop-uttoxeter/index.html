Have questions about our garden furniture collection? Here are some frequently asked questions and answers to help you make informed decisions.

Question 1: What materials are used in your garden furniture?
Answer: We use a variety of high-quality materials in our garden furniture, including durable woods like teak and acacia, weather-resistant metals like aluminum and stainless steel, and synthetic materials like resin wicker and textilene. These materials are chosen for their strength, durability, and resistance to the elements.

Question 2: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for your garden furniture depends on the materials used. Teak furniture requires regular oiling to maintain its natural beauty and protect it from the elements. Metal furniture can be cleaned with mild soap and water. Synthetic materials like resin wicker and textilene are generally low-maintenance and can be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Question 3: Can I leave my garden furniture outdoors all year round?
Answer: Some of our garden furniture is designed to withstand outdoor use all year round, while others may require protection from harsh weather conditions. It's best to check the specific care instructions for your furniture to determine whether it can be left outdoors.

Tips

When choosing and using garden furniture, there are a few practical tips to keep in mind to ensure you make the most of your outdoor space and create a comfortable and stylish environment.

Tip 1: Consider Your Needs and Lifestyle:

Think about how you plan to use your garden furniture. Will it be primarily for dining, lounging, or entertaining guests? Choose pieces that suit your needs and lifestyle. If you have a small space, consider multifunctional furniture that can serve multiple purposes.

Tip 2: Choose Durable and Weather-Resistant Materials:

Garden furniture is exposed to various weather conditions, so it's important to choose materials that can withstand the elements. Look for furniture made from durable materials like teak, aluminum, or synthetic materials like resin wicker and textilene.

Tip 3: Comfort is Key:

Comfort is essential when it comes to garden furniture. Choose pieces that provide adequate support and cushioning. Consider adding outdoor cushions and pillows for extra comfort and style.

Tip 4: Create a Cohesive Look:

When selecting garden furniture, consider the overall aesthetic you want to achieve. Choose pieces that complement each other in terms of style, color, and material. You can also add accessories like outdoor rugs, cushions, and planters to enhance the look and feel of your outdoor space.
