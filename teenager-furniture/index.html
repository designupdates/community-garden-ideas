Question 1: What are the different types of garden furniture?
Answer 1: Garden furniture comes in a variety of styles, materials, and designs. Some of the most popular types of garden furniture include:

  • Wooden garden furniture: Wooden garden furniture is classic and stylish. It is made from durable hardwoods such as teak, cedar, or redwood.
  • Metal garden furniture: Metal garden furniture is strong and lightweight. It is made from aluminum, wrought iron, or steel.
  • Plastic garden furniture: Plastic garden furniture is affordable and easy to maintain. It is made from durable plastics such as polyethylene or polypropylene.
  • Wicker garden furniture: Wicker garden furniture is stylish and comfortable. It is made from natural materials such as rattan, bamboo, or willow.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
