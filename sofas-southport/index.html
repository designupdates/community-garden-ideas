Measure your space

Before you start shopping for a sofa, it's important to measure your space. This will help you determine what size sofa will fit comfortably in your room. You don't want to end up with a sofa that's too big or too small.

To measure your space, you'll need to measure the length and width of the area where you want to put the sofa. You should also measure the height of the ceiling, in case you want a sofa with a high back.

Once you have the measurements, you can start shopping for a sofa. When you're looking at sofas, pay attention to the dimensions. Make sure that the sofa will fit comfortably in your space.

It's also important to consider the traffic flow in your room. You don't want to place the sofa in a way that blocks the flow of traffic. Make sure that there is enough space to walk around the sofa and to get to other furniture in the room.

By measuring your space before you start shopping, you can be sure that you'll find the perfect sofa for your Southport home.
