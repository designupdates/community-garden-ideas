Easy to apply

Yellow furniture paint is easy to apply, even for beginners. It can be applied with a brush, roller, or sprayer, and it dries quickly and evenly. Here are a few tips for applying yellow furniture paint:

2. Sand the surface of the furniture lightly to create a smooth surface for the paint to adhere to. You can use a fine-grit sandpaper (120-grit or higher) and sand in the direction of the grain.

3. Apply a primer to the furniture. This will help the paint to adhere better and will prevent the color of the wood from bleeding through.

4. Apply the yellow paint in thin, even coats. Allow each coat to dry completely before applying the next. You may need to apply 2-3 coats to achieve the desired coverage.
