Durable and sturdy

Aluminium rattan garden furniture is designed to withstand the elements and last for many years to come. Here are four reasons why it is so durable and sturdy:

  • Aluminium frame: The frames of aluminium rattan garden furniture are made of high-quality aluminium, which is a strong and lightweight metal. Aluminium is resistant to rust and corrosion, making it ideal for outdoor use.
  • Powder-coated finish: The aluminium frames are powder-coated, which further protects them from the elements. Powder-coating is a process in which a dry powder is applied to the metal and then cured at a high temperature. This creates a durable and long-lasting finish that is resistant to chipping, peeling, and fading.
  • Rattan wicker: The rattan wicker used in aluminium rattan garden furniture is made of high-density polyethylene (HDPE). HDPE is a strong and durable plastic that is resistant to UV rays and moisture. It is also colourfast, meaning that it will not fade over time.
  • Expert craftsmanship: Aluminium rattan garden furniture is made by skilled craftsmen who use high-quality materials and construction techniques. This ensures that the furniture is built to last and can withstand regular use.

As a result of its durable and sturdy construction, aluminium rattan garden furniture is a great investment for anyone who wants to enjoy their outdoor space for many years to come.
