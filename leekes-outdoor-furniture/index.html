Here are some specific examples of how Leekes outdoor furniture has been tested for durability and weather-resistance:

  • Fade resistance: Our fabrics are tested for fade resistance according to the AATCC 16.3 standard. This test involves exposing the fabrics to UV radiation for 1,000 hours, which is equivalent to several years of outdoor use. Our fabrics have been shown to retain their color and vibrancy even after this extensive exposure.
  • Water repellency: Our fabrics are tested for water repellency according to the AATCC 118 standard. This test involves spraying water onto the fabrics and measuring how much water is absorbed. Our fabrics have been shown to repel water effectively, preventing it from soaking in and damaging the cushions or upholstery.
  • Wind resistance: Our furniture is tested for wind resistance according to the ANSI/BIFMA X5.1 standard. This test involves exposing the furniture to winds of up to 55 mph. Our furniture has been shown to withstand these winds without tipping over or being damaged.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What are the different types of garden furniture?
Answer: Garden furniture comes in a variety of types, including chairs, tables, benches, loungers, and umbrellas. It can be made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker.

Question 2: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. Wood is a classic choice that is both durable and stylish. Metal is another durable option that is also relatively easy to care for. Plastic is a more affordable option that is available in a variety of colors and styles. Wicker is a natural material that is both durable and comfortable.

Question 3: How do I choose the right garden furniture for my needs?
Answer: When choosing garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your space, the style of your home, and your personal needs. You should also consider the materials used to make the furniture and the level of maintenance required.

Question 4: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to follow the manufacturer's instructions. In general, wood furniture should be oiled or sealed regularly to protect it from the elements. Metal furniture should be wiped down with a damp cloth to remove dirt and debris. Plastic furniture can be cleaned with a mild soap solution. Wicker furniture should be brushed regularly to remove dirt and debris.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: Whether or not you can leave your garden furniture outside all year round depends on the materials used to make the furniture and the climate in your area. Wood furniture is not typically recommended for outdoor use in cold climates, as it can be damaged by freezing temperatures. Metal furniture is more durable, but it can still be damaged by rust if it is not properly cared for. Plastic furniture is the most durable option for outdoor use, as it is not affected by the elements.

Question 6: Where can I buy garden furniture?
Answer: Garden furniture can be purchased at a variety of stores, including home improvement stores, furniture stores, and online retailers. It is important to compare prices and read reviews before making a purchase.

Tips

Here are four tips for choosing and caring for garden furniture:

1. Choose the right material for your needs. The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. Wood is a classic choice that is both durable and stylish. Metal is another durable option that is also relatively easy to care for. However, metal is not the best choice for areas with frequent rain, as it can rust easily. Aluminum is a lightweight metal that is weather- and rust-proof, making it a good choice for outdoor areas exposed to the elements. Wicker is a natural material that is both durable and comfortable. However, wicker can be more expensive than other materials, and it can be damaged by moisture.

2. Consider the size of your space. When choosing garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your space. You don't want to choose furniture that is too large or too small for your space. If your space is small, you may want to choose smaller pieces of furniture that can be easily moved around. If your space is large, you can choose larger pieces of furniture that will provide more comfortable and stylish ways to relax and entertain guests.

3. Think about your style. The style of your garden furniture should match the style of your home and your personal taste. If you have a traditional home, you may want to choose garden furniture with a classic design. If you have a modern home, you may want to choose garden furniture with a more contemporary design. If you have a coastal home, you may want to choose garden furniture with a relaxed and beachy style.

4. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for care. The best way to care for your garden furniture is to follow the manufacturer's instructions. In general, wood furniture should be oiled or sealed regularly to protect it from the elements. Metal furniture should be wiped down with a damp cloth to remove dirt and moisture, and it may need to be re-painted or powder-coated periodically to protect it from rust.
