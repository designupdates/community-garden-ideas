Traditional techniques

Millhouse Furniture takes great pride in its commitment to traditional techniques, which have been passed down from generation to generation. These techniques have stood the test of time, resulting in furniture that is not only beautiful but also incredibly durable.

One of the most important traditional techniques used by Millhouse Furniture is joinery. Joinery is the art of connecting pieces of wood together without the use of nails or screws. Millhouse Furniture's artisans are experts in various joinery techniques, including mortise and tenon joints, dovetail joints, and dowel joints. These techniques ensure that Millhouse Furniture pieces are incredibly strong and durable.

Another traditional technique used by Millhouse Furniture is hand-carving. Hand-carving adds a level of detail and beauty to furniture that is simply not possible with machine-made pieces. Millhouse Furniture's artisans use a variety of chisels and gouges to create intricate carvings that add a touch of elegance and sophistication to their furniture.

Millhouse Furniture also uses traditional techniques in its upholstery work. Their upholsterers are skilled in the art of hand-stitching, which creates a more durable and comfortable seat than machine-stitching. They also use high-quality materials, such as horsehair and down, to ensure that their upholstered furniture is both comfortable and supportive.

FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got answers! Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about garden furniture, along with their answers:

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Some popular materials include teak, acacia, metal, and wicker. Teak and acacia are both durable hardwoods that are naturally resistant to rot and decay. Metal furniture is also durable and easy to care for, but it can be hot to the touch in direct sunlight. Wicker furniture is lightweight and airy, making it a good choice for small spaces.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer 2: When choosing garden furniture, it's important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You also need to think about how you plan to use the furniture. If you're looking for a place to relax and enjoy the outdoors, you might want to choose a comfortable lounge set. If you're planning on hosting large gatherings, you might need a dining set or a larger seating area.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 3: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to follow the manufacturer's instructions. Some general care tips include: cleaning the furniture regularly with mild soap and water, protecting it from harsh weather conditions, and storing it in a dry place during the off-season.

Question 4: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer 4: There are a few things you can do to make your garden furniture more comfortable. Add cushions and pillows to your seating furniture, and use throws and blankets to keep warm on cooler evenings. You can also add an outdoor rug to define your space and make it more inviting.

Question 5: What are some popular garden furniture trends?
Answer 5: Some popular garden furniture trends include: using natural materials, such as wood and wicker, incorporating bright colors and patterns, and choosing furniture that is both stylish and functional. You can also add personal touches to your garden furniture, such as painting it or adding your own cushions and pillows.

Question 6: Where can I buy garden furniture?
