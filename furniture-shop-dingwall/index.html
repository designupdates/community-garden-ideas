Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. However, some of the most popular materials for garden furniture include:

  • Teak: Teak is a durable hardwood that is naturally resistant to rot and decay. It is also a relatively low-maintenance material, making it a good choice for busy homeowners.
  • Redwood: Redwood is another durable hardwood that is resistant to rot and decay. It is also a lightweight material, making it easy to move around.
  • Plastic: Plastic is a lightweight and affordable material that is available in a variety of colors and styles. However, plastic furniture can be less durable than other materials, and it may fade or become brittle over time.
  • Metal: Metal is a durable material that is available in a variety of finishes. However, metal furniture can be heavy and it can rust if it is not properly maintained.
