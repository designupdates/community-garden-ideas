A History of Excellence:

The Hardys Furniture story began in 1905 when Samuel Hardy, a skilled cabinetmaker, founded a modest furniture workshop in the town of Ashville, North Carolina. With a passion for creating beautiful and functional pieces, Samuel dedicated himself to producing furniture that showcased the finest materials and meticulous attention to detail. As his reputation grew, so did the demand for his handcrafted furniture, leading to the establishment of the Hardys Furniture Company in 1920.

From humble beginnings to a global presence, Hardys Furniture continues to uphold its commitment to providing exceptional products and services.

FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? Here are some frequently asked questions and answers to help you:

Question 1: What factors should I consider when choosing garden furniture?

Answer 1: When choosing garden furniture, consider factors such as the size of your outdoor space, the purpose of the furniture (dining, lounging, etc.), the materials used (durability and weather resistance), the comfort and style of the furniture, and your budget.

Question 2: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?

Answer 2: To protect your garden furniture from the elements, consider using weather-resistant covers, cushions, and sealants. Store furniture indoors or under a covered area during harsh weather conditions.

Question 3: What are the different types of materials used for garden furniture?

Answer 3: Common materials used for garden furniture include wood (teak, acacia, eucalyptus), metal (aluminum, iron, stainless steel), wicker (natural or synthetic), plastic (polyethylene, polypropylene), and concrete.

Question 4: How can I clean and maintain my garden furniture?

Answer 4: Regularly clean your garden furniture with mild soap and water. Use specific cleaning solutions for different materials. Apply protective sealants or oils to wood furniture to maintain its appearance and durability.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outdoors all year round?

Answer 5: The durability of your garden furniture depends on the materials used and the climate in your area. Some furniture is designed to withstand outdoor conditions, while others may need to be stored indoors during harsh weather.

Question 6: Where can I find quality garden furniture at affordable prices?

Answer 6: You can find quality garden furniture at affordable prices at reputable furniture stores, online retailers, and home improvement centers. Consider comparing prices and reading reviews before making a purchase.

These frequently asked questions and answers provide a starting point for your garden furniture journey. Remember to consider your specific needs, preferences, and budget when making your choices. Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you choose and maintain your garden furniture:

Tip 1: Consider Your Space and Needs

Before selecting garden furniture, carefully assess the size and layout of your outdoor space. Choose furniture that is proportionate to the area and consider the intended purpose (dining, lounging, etc.). Measure the space and make a plan to ensure that the furniture fits comfortably.

Tip 2: Choose Durable and Weather-Resistant Materials

Garden furniture is exposed to various weather conditions, so choosing durable and weather-resistant materials is crucial. Consider materials like teak, acacia, aluminum, or synthetic wicker that can withstand outdoor elements. Look for furniture with UV protection to prevent fading and damage from the sun.

Tip 3: Prioritize Comfort and Style

Garden furniture should be both comfortable and stylish to enhance your outdoor experience. Choose furniture with comfortable cushions and ergonomic designs. Consider the overall style of your outdoor space and select furniture that complements your décor and personal taste.

Tip 4: Maintain Your Garden Furniture Regularly

Regular maintenance is essential to extend the lifespan of your garden furniture. Clean the furniture periodically with mild soap and water. Use specific cleaning solutions for different materials. Apply protective sealants or oils to wood furniture to maintain its appearance and durability. Store furniture indoors or under a covered area during harsh weather conditions.

Closing Paragraph for Tips

By following these tips, you can choose and maintain garden furniture that suits your needs, enhances the beauty of your outdoor space, and provides years of enjoyment.
