  • Dents

    Dents can be repaired using a variety of methods, depending on the size and depth of the dent. For small dents, you can use a steam iron to gently lift the dent out. For larger dents, you may need to use a wood filler or epoxy. Once the filler has dried, you can sand it smooth and apply a new finish.

  • Other damage

    Wood restorer can also be used to repair other types of damage, such as cracks, chips, and holes. The best method for repairing these types of damage will depend on the specific situation. However, wood restorer can be a great way to make your furniture look like new again.

Available in a variety of types

Wood restorer for furniture is available in a variety of types, so you can choose the one that best suits your needs. The most common types of wood restorer include:

    Liquid wood restorer is a liquid that is applied to the damaged area of the furniture. It is then allowed to penetrate the wood and repair the damage. Liquid wood restorer is available in a variety of colors, so you can choose the one that best matches your furniture.

  • Paste wood restorer

    Paste wood restorer is a paste that is applied to the damaged area of the furniture. It is then allowed to dry and harden. Paste wood restorer is available in a variety of colors, so you can choose the one that best matches your furniture.

  • Stick wood restorer

    Stick wood restorer is a stick that is rubbed onto the damaged area of the furniture. It is then allowed to dry and harden. Stick wood restorer is available in a variety of colors, so you can choose the one that best matches your furniture.

  • Powder wood restorer

    Powder wood restorer is a powder that is mixed with water to create a paste. The paste is then applied to the damaged area of the furniture. It is then allowed to dry and harden. Powder wood restorer is available in a variety of colors, so you can choose the one that best matches your furniture.
