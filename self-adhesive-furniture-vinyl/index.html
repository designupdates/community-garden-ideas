Easy to apply

One of the best things about self-adhesive furniture vinyl is that it is very easy to apply. You don't need any special tools or skills, and you can do it yourself in just a few minutes.

  • Clean the surface: The first step is to clean the surface of the furniture that you want to cover. Make sure that it is clean and free of any dust or dirt.
  • Measure and cut: Next, you need to measure and cut the vinyl to the size of the furniture. Be sure to measure twice and cut once, so that you don't waste any material.
  • Peel and stick: Once you have cut the vinyl, peel off the backing and stick it to the furniture. Be sure to smooth out any bubbles or wrinkles as you go.
  • Trim the edges: Once the vinyl is applied, you can trim the edges with a sharp knife or scissors. This will give your furniture a clean and finished look.

That's all there is to it! With self-adhesive furniture vinyl, you can easily give your old furniture a new look in just a few minutes.
