Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. Some of the most popular materials include teak, unsalted wrought iron, aluminum, and plastic. Teak is a durable hardwood that is resistant to rot and decay, while wrought iron is a sturdy and long-lasting material that can withstand harsh weather conditions. Aluminum is a lightweight and rust-resistant material that is easy to maintain, and plastic is a budget-friendly option that is available in a variety of colors and styles.
