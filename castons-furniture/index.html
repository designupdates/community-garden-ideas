Tip 2: Measure your space.

Before you start shopping for garden furniture, measure the space where you plan to put it. This will help you choose furniture that is the right size and scale for your space.

Tip 3: Consider your style.

Garden furniture comes in a variety of styles, from traditional to contemporary. Choose furniture that matches the style of your home and garden.
