High-quality rattan material

When choosing 6-seater rattan garden furniture on clearance, it's crucial to consider the quality of the rattan material. High-quality rattan is durable, weather-resistant, and can withstand the elements for many years to come.

Look for rattan furniture made from natural materials such as wicker, bamboo, or polyethylene (PE) rattan. These materials are known for their strength and resistance to fading, cracking, and peeling.

Natural rattan is a sustainable choice as it is a renewable resource. It is also biodegradable and recyclable, making it an environmentally friendly option.

PE rattan, on the other hand, is a synthetic material that mimics the look and feel of natural rattan. It is highly durable and resistant to water, UV rays, and insects, making it ideal for outdoor use.

By choosing 6-seater rattan garden furniture made from high-quality materials, you can ensure that your furniture will last for many years and provide you with years of enjoyment.

Low maintenance and easy to clean

Rattan furniture is incredibly low maintenance and easy to clean. Unlike wood or metal furniture, rattan does not require any special treatment or maintenance. Here are a few tips for keeping your rattan furniture looking its best:

  • Clean regularly

    To keep your rattan furniture clean, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth or sponge. You can also use a mild soap solution if necessary. Rinse the furniture thoroughly with clean water and allow it to dry completely.

  • Brush away dirt and debris

    If your rattan furniture gets dirty or dusty, you can brush away the dirt and debris with a soft-bristled brush. Be sure to brush in the direction of the grain.

  • Protect from the elements

    If possible, protect your rattan furniture from the elements. Avoid leaving it in direct sunlight or rain for extended periods of time. If your furniture does get wet, allow it to dry completely before using it again.

  • Store indoors during winter

    If you live in a climate with cold winters, it's best to store your rattan furniture indoors during the winter months. This will help to protect it from the elements and extend its lifespan.

By following these simple tips, you can keep your 6-seater rattan garden furniture looking its best for years to come.
