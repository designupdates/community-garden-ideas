FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got you covered. Here are some of the most frequently asked questions we receive:

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best materials for garden furniture are those that are durable, weather-resistant, and easy to maintain. Some of the most popular materials include teak, aluminum, wicker, and plastic.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer 2: When choosing the right size garden furniture, it's important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You should also think about the type of activities you'll be using the furniture for, such as dining, lounging, or entertaining.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 3: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You should also protect it from the elements by covering it when not in use.

Question 4: How long will my garden furniture last?
Answer 4: The lifespan of your garden furniture will vary depending on the materials used and the level of care you provide. However, with proper care, most garden furniture will last for many years.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer 5: Whether or not you can leave your garden furniture outside all year round will depend on the climate you live in and the materials your furniture is made from. If you live in a cold climate, it's best to store your furniture indoors during the winter months.

Question 6: How do I assemble my garden furniture?
Answer 6: Most garden furniture comes with clear assembly instructions. If you have any difficulties, you can always contact the manufacturer for assistance.
