FAQ

Our FAQ section is designed to answer some of the most common questions you may have about garden furniture. Whether you're looking for tips on choosing the right pieces or want to know how to care for your furniture, we've got you covered.

Question 1: How do I choose the right garden furniture?
Answer 1: When choosing garden furniture, consider factors like the size of your space, the purpose of the furniture (dining, lounging, etc.), and your personal style. You'll also want to think about the material the furniture is made from and its durability in different weather conditions.

Question 2: What are the different types of garden furniture materials?
Answer 2: Common garden furniture materials include wood (teak, acacia, and eucalyptus), metal (aluminum and wrought iron), wicker (natural and synthetic), and plastic (polyethylene and PVC).

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 3: Caring for your garden furniture will help extend its lifespan. Regularly clean it with a mild detergent and water solution, and apply protective treatments or sealants according to the manufacturer's instructions.

Question 4: Can I leave my garden furniture outdoors all year round?
Answer 4: Whether you can leave your garden furniture outdoors all year round depends on the material it's made from and the climate in your area. Some materials, like teak and aluminum, are more resistant to weather damage than others. If you live in an area with harsh winters, it's best to store your furniture indoors or cover it with a protective cover.

Question 5: How can I create a cohesive look with my garden furniture?
Answer 5: To create a cohesive look with your garden furniture, consider choosing pieces from the same collection or with similar design elements. You can also use cushions, throws, and other accessories to tie the look together.
