FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got answers! Explore our frequently asked questions to help you make informed decisions and create your perfect outdoor oasis.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer: The best materials for garden furniture are those that are weather-resistant and durable. Consider materials such as teak, acacia wood, aluminum, and synthetic rattan, which can withstand the elements and last for many seasons.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: Measure the available space in your garden and consider the number of people you typically entertain. Choose furniture that is proportionate to the size of your space, leaving enough room for comfortable movement.

Question 3: What style of garden furniture should I choose?
Answer: The style of garden furniture you choose should complement the overall aesthetic of your outdoor space. Consider traditional styles, such as Victorian or wrought iron, or opt for more modern and contemporary designs. Choose pieces that reflect your personal taste and create a cohesive look.

Question 4: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: Regular cleaning and maintenance will extend the life of your garden furniture. Clean furniture with a mild soap solution and water, and apply a protective sealant or oil to wooden furniture to prevent weathering.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: The durability of garden furniture varies depending on the material. Some materials, such as teak and aluminum, can withstand year-round exposure to the elements. However, it's generally recommended to store furniture indoors or cover it during harsh weather conditions.

Question 6: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture?
Answer: Kingston Upon Thames offers a wide range of furniture stores that specialize in garden furniture. Visit showrooms, browse online catalogs, and compare prices to find the best options for your needs and budget.

With these FAQs answered, you're well on your way to creating a beautiful and functional outdoor space with garden furniture that you'll cherish for years to come.
