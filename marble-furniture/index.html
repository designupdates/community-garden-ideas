Easy to clean and maintain

Marble furniture is relatively easy to clean and maintain. Here are a few tips for keeping your marble furniture looking its best:

  • Use a soft cloth: When cleaning marble furniture, always use a soft cloth. Avoid using abrasive cloths or sponges, as these can scratch the surface of the marble.
  • Use a mild detergent: If your marble furniture is dirty, you can clean it with a mild detergent and water. Avoid using harsh chemicals, as these can damage the marble.
  • Rinse thoroughly: After cleaning your marble furniture, be sure to rinse it thoroughly with water. This will remove any soap residue and help to prevent streaks.
  • Dry immediately: After rinsing your marble furniture, dry it immediately with a soft cloth. This will help to prevent water spots.

In addition to regular cleaning, you should also seal your marble furniture periodically. Sealing will help to protect the marble from stains and damage. You can purchase a marble sealer at most home improvement stores.
