Tips

Here are four practical tips for choosing and caring for garden furniture:

Before you start shopping for garden furniture, measure your outdoor space to determine how much furniture you can fit. This will help you avoid buying furniture that is too large or too small for your space.

Tip 2: Choose furniture that is made from durable materials.
Garden furniture is exposed to the elements, so it's important to choose furniture that is made from durable materials. Look for furniture that is made from weather-resistant materials, such as teak, aluminum, or plastic.

Tip 3: Consider your comfort.
