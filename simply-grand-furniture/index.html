FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got answers! Check out our frequently asked questions below:

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer: The best materials for garden furniture are those that can withstand the elements, such as teak, cedar, and aluminum.

Question 2: How do I maintain my garden furniture?
Answer: Regular cleaning and maintenance will help to extend the life of your garden furniture. Clean your furniture with a mild soap and water solution, and be sure to dry it thoroughly.

Question 3: How do I choose the right size garden furniture for my space?
Answer: When choosing garden furniture, it's important to consider the size of your space. Measure your space before you shop, and be sure to leave enough room for people to move around comfortably.

Question 4: What style of garden furniture is right for me?
Answer: The style of garden furniture you choose will depend on your personal taste. There are many different styles to choose from, so take your time and browse until you find something you love.

Question 5: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: You can protect your garden furniture from the elements by using a furniture cover. Furniture covers are available in a variety of materials, so you can choose one that is right for your needs.

Question 6: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: It is not recommended to leave your garden furniture outside all year round. The elements can damage your furniture over time, so it is best to store it in a dry place during the winter months.

Question 7: How do I clean my garden furniture cushions?
Answer: To clean your garden furniture cushions, remove the covers and machine wash them in cold water. Be sure to air dry the cushions completely before putting them back on your furniture.
