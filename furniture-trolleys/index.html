Answer 2: There are a few things you can do to protect your garden furniture from the elements. First, choose furniture that is made from materials that are weather-resistant. Second, apply a sealant or protector to the furniture to help repel water and UV rays. Third, store your furniture indoors or under a cover when it is not in use. Question 3: How can I clean my garden furniture?
Answer 3: The best way to clean your garden furniture depends on the material it is made from. For wood furniture, use a mild detergent and water.
