Sell Office Furniture Online: A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jan 28, 2029 Â· 11 min read

Selling office furniture online can be an effective way to declutter, save money, and even make a profit. With the rise of online marketplaces like Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, and eBay, it's easier than ever to find buyers for your used furniture.

This comprehensive guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to sell office furniture online. We'll cover everything from pricing and listing your items to shipping and handling payments.

Before we dive into the details, let's start with some general tips for selling office furniture online successfully.

office furniture to sell

When selling office furniture online, it's important to keep a few key points in mind:

  • Take high-quality photos
  • Write detailed descriptions
  • Price your items competitively
  • Offer multiple shipping options
  • Be responsive to inquiries
  • Provide excellent customer service

By following these tips, you can increase your chances of selling your office furniture quickly and easily.

Take high-quality photos

When selling office furniture online, the quality of your photos can make a big difference in whether or not someone decides to buy your item. High-quality photos will show off your furniture's best features and make it more appealing to potential buyers.

Here are a few tips for taking great photos of your office furniture:

  • Use natural light. If possible, take your photos in a well-lit room with plenty of natural light. This will help your photos look bright and inviting.
  • Take multiple photos from different angles. This will give potential buyers a good sense of the size and shape of your furniture.
  • Close up on details. Take close-up photos of any unique features or details of your furniture. This will help buyers see the quality of your workmanship.
  • Edit your photos. Once you've taken your photos, take some time to edit them. Crop out any unnecessary background elements and adjust the brightness and contrast to make your furniture look its best.

By following these tips, you can take high-quality photos that will help you sell your office furniture online quickly and easily.

In addition to the tips above, here are a few more things to keep in mind when taking photos of your office furniture:

  • Make sure your furniture is clean and free of dust and debris.
  • Stage your furniture in a way that makes it look appealing. Add a few decorative touches, such as a plant or a vase of flowers.
  • Use a high-quality camera or smartphone to take your photos. The better the quality of your photos, the more likely you are to sell your furniture.

Write detailed descriptions

In addition to taking high-quality photos, writing detailed descriptions of your office furniture is also important. Your descriptions should be informative and engaging, and they should highlight the best features of your furniture.

Here are a few tips for writing great descriptions for your office furniture:

  • Start with a strong headline. Your headline is the first thing potential buyers will see, so make it attention-grabbing and informative.
  • Use descriptive language. Paint a picture of your furniture in the buyer's mind. Use words that describe the size, shape, color, and style of your furniture.
  • Highlight the unique features of your furniture. What makes your furniture special? Is it made from high-quality materials? Does it have any unique features or details? Be sure to mention these things in your description.
  • Use keywords. When writing your description, be sure to use keywords that potential buyers are likely to search for. This will help your listing appear in search results.
  • Proofread your description. Before you publish your listing, be sure to proofread your description carefully for any errors.

By following these tips, you can write detailed descriptions that will help you sell your office furniture online quickly and easily.

In addition to the tips above, here are a few more things to keep in mind when writing descriptions for your office furniture:

  • Be honest and accurate in your descriptions. Don't exaggerate the condition or quality of your furniture.
  • Use a friendly and conversational tone in your descriptions. This will make your listing more inviting to potential buyers.
  • Keep your descriptions concise and to the point. Potential buyers are more likely to read short, easy-to-read descriptions.

Price your items competitively

Pricing your office furniture competitively is one of the most important factors in selling it quickly and easily. If you price your furniture too high, potential buyers will be less likely to purchase it. If you price it too low, you'll end up losing money.

Here are a few tips for pricing your office furniture competitively:

  • Research the market. Before you price your furniture, take some time to research the market. See what similar items are selling for on online marketplaces like Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist.
  • Consider the condition of your furniture. If your furniture is in good condition, you can price it higher than if it's in poor condition.
  • Factor in the cost of shipping. If you're planning to ship your furniture, be sure to factor in the cost of shipping when pricing your items.
  • Be willing to negotiate. Most buyers will expect to negotiate the price of your furniture. Be prepared to come down on your price if necessary.

By following these tips, you can price your office furniture competitively and increase your chances of selling it quickly and easily.

Here are a few additional tips for pricing your office furniture competitively:

  • Use round numbers when pricing your items. For example, instead of pricing your desk at $125, price it at $120.
  • Offer discounts for multiple purchases. If you're selling multiple pieces of furniture, offer a discount to buyers who purchase more than one item.
  • Be open to offers. Even if you've priced your furniture competitively, be open to offers from buyers. You may be able to get a higher price than you originally expected.

Offer multiple shipping options

Offering multiple shipping options is a great way to make it easier for potential buyers to purchase your office furniture. By giving buyers a choice of shipping methods, you can increase your chances of making a sale.

Here are a few of the most popular shipping options you can offer:

  • Local pickup. This is a great option for buyers who live nearby and are willing to pick up the furniture themselves.
  • Standard shipping. This is the most common shipping method and typically takes 3-5 business days.
  • Expedited shipping. This is a faster shipping method and typically takes 1-2 business days.
  • White glove delivery. This is a premium shipping method that includes delivery and assembly of your furniture.

The shipping options you offer will depend on the size and weight of your furniture, as well as your budget. Be sure to factor in the cost of shipping when pricing your items.

Here are a few additional tips for offering multiple shipping options:

  • Be clear about the shipping costs for each option.
  • Offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount.
  • Package your furniture carefully to avoid damage during shipping.
  • Provide tracking information to buyers so they can track the status of their order.

Be responsive to inquiries

When selling office furniture online, it's important to be responsive to inquiries from potential buyers. This means responding to questions promptly and providing helpful information. The faster you respond to inquiries, the more likely you are to make a sale.

  • Respond to inquiries within 24 hours. This shows potential buyers that you're serious about selling your furniture and that you're willing to answer their questions.
  • Provide detailed answers to questions. Don't just give one-word answers. Take the time to provide helpful information that will help potential buyers make a decision.
  • Be honest and upfront about the condition of your furniture. Don't try to hide any flaws or damage. Be honest about the condition of your furniture so that potential buyers know what they're getting into.
  • Be willing to negotiate. Most buyers will expect to negotiate the price of your furniture. Be prepared to come down on your price if necessary.

By following these tips, you can be responsive to inquiries from potential buyers and increase your chances of selling your office furniture quickly and easily.

Provide excellent customer service

Providing excellent customer service is essential for selling office furniture online. When you provide great customer service, buyers are more likely to be satisfied with their purchase and leave positive feedback.

  • Respond to inquiries promptly and professionally. This shows buyers that you're serious about selling your furniture and that you're willing to answer their questions.
  • Be honest and upfront about the condition of your furniture. Don't try to hide any flaws or damage. Be honest about the condition of your furniture so that buyers know what they're getting into.
  • Package your furniture carefully to avoid damage during shipping. This shows buyers that you care about their purchase and that you want them to be happy with it.
  • Follow up with buyers after the sale to make sure they're satisfied. This shows buyers that you care about their satisfaction and that you're willing to go the extra mile to make sure they're happy.

By following these tips, you can provide excellent customer service and increase your chances of selling your office furniture quickly and easily.
