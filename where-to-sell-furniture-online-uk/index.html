Where to Sell Furniture Online UK

Written by William Mar 01, 2029 · 11 min read

Are you looking to declutter your home and make some extra cash? Selling your unwanted furniture online is a great way to do just that. But with so many different online marketplaces to choose from, it can be hard to know where to start.

In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on where to sell furniture online in the UK. We'll cover everything from the most popular marketplaces to niche websites and apps. We'll also provide you with tips on how to price your furniture, take great photos, and write effective listings.

Now that you know where to sell your furniture online, it's time to start preparing your listings.

Where to Sell Furniture Online UK

Here are 8 important points to consider when selling furniture online in the UK:

  • Choose the right marketplace
  • Price your furniture competitively
  • Take great photos
  • Write effective listings
  • Offer delivery or collection
  • Be prepared to negotiate
  • Provide excellent customer service
  • Follow up with buyers

By following these tips, you can increase your chances of selling your furniture quickly and for a good price.

Choose the right marketplace

The first step to selling your furniture online is to choose the right marketplace. There are a number of different marketplaces to choose from, each with its own advantages and disadvantages.

  • eBay

    eBay is one of the most popular online marketplaces in the UK. It's a great option for selling furniture because it has a large audience of potential buyers. However, eBay can be competitive, and you may need to price your furniture lower to sell it quickly.

  • Facebook Marketplace

    Facebook Marketplace is a great option for selling furniture locally. It's easy to use and free to list your items. However, Facebook Marketplace can be less competitive than eBay, so you may be able to get a higher price for your furniture.

  • Gumtree

    Gumtree is another popular online marketplace in the UK. It's a good option for selling furniture because it's free to list your items and you can reach a large audience of potential buyers. However, Gumtree can be less competitive than eBay and Facebook Marketplace, so you may need to price your furniture lower to sell it quickly.

  • Shpock

    Shpock is a mobile-first marketplace that's great for selling furniture locally. It's easy to use and free to list your items. However, Shpock can be less competitive than eBay, Facebook Marketplace, and Gumtree, so you may need to price your furniture lower to sell it quickly.

Once you've chosen a marketplace, you can start creating your listings. Be sure to take great photos, write effective listings, and price your furniture competitively.

Price your furniture competitively

One of the most important factors in selling your furniture online is pricing it competitively. If you price your furniture too high, it will be less likely to sell. If you price it too low, you will lose out on potential profits.

The best way to price your furniture is to research similar items that have recently sold on the marketplace you are using. You can do this by searching for the item on the marketplace and filtering the results by "sold listings." Once you have a good idea of what similar items are selling for, you can price your furniture accordingly.

Here are some additional tips for pricing your furniture competitively:

  • Consider the condition of your furniture. If your furniture is in good condition, you can price it higher than if it is in poor condition.
  • Factor in the cost of delivery. If you are offering delivery, you need to factor the cost of delivery into your pricing.
  • Be willing to negotiate. Most buyers will expect to be able to negotiate on the price of your furniture. Be prepared to lower your price if necessary to close the deal.

By following these tips, you can increase your chances of selling your furniture quickly and for a good price.

Offer delivery or collection

Once you have priced your furniture and taken great photos, it's time to write effective listings. Your listings should be clear, concise, and informative. They should also be persuasive, so that potential buyers will want to buy your furniture.

  • Use a clear and concise title. Your title should accurately describe your furniture and include the most important information, such as the type of furniture, the brand, and the condition.
  • Write a detailed description. Your description should provide potential buyers with all the information they need to make a decision about whether or not to buy your furniture. Include information about the furniture's condition, dimensions, materials, and any other relevant details.
  • Use high-quality photos. Your photos should be clear and well-lit, and they should show your furniture from all angles. Potential buyers will be more likely to buy your furniture if they can see exactly what they're getting.
  • Set a competitive price. Your price should be competitive with other similar items on the market. You can research similar items on the marketplace you are using to get an idea of what they are selling for.

Once you have written effective listings, you can start promoting your furniture to potential buyers.

Be prepared to negotiate

When selling furniture online, it's important to be prepared to negotiate. Most buyers will expect to be able to haggle on the price, so be prepared to lower your price if necessary. However, you should also be firm on your price and not be afraid to walk away from a deal if the buyer is not willing to pay your asking price.

  • Be prepared to negotiate on the price. Most buyers will expect to be able to haggle on the price, so be prepared to lower your price if necessary. However, you should also be firm on your price and not be afraid to walk away from a deal if the buyer is not willing to pay your asking price.
  • Be prepared to negotiate on the delivery. If you are offering delivery, be prepared to negotiate on the delivery fee. You may be able to offer free delivery if the buyer is willing to pick up the furniture from your home.
  • Be prepared to negotiate on the condition of the furniture. If the furniture is not in perfect condition, be prepared to negotiate on the price. You may be able to sell the furniture for a lower price if the buyer is willing to accept the furniture as is.

By being prepared to negotiate, you can increase your chances of selling your furniture quickly and for a good price.

Provide excellent customer service

Providing excellent customer service is key to selling furniture online. Here are a few tips:

  • Respond to inquiries quickly. When a potential buyer sends you an inquiry, respond as quickly as possible. This shows that you are interested in their business and that you are responsive to their needs.
  • Be friendly and helpful. When you are communicating with potential buyers, be friendly and helpful. Answer their questions honestly and thoroughly, and be willing to go the extra mile to help them make a decision.
  • Be patient. Selling furniture online can take time. Be patient with potential buyers and don't pressure them to make a decision. Give them the time they need to consider their options and make a decision.
  • Follow up with buyers. After a potential buyer has purchased your furniture, follow up with them to make sure they are happy with their purchase. This shows that you care about your customers and that you are committed to providing excellent customer service.

By providing excellent customer service, you can increase your chances of selling your furniture quickly and for a good price. You can also build a loyal customer base that will be more likely to buy from you again in the future.

Follow up with buyers

Following up with buyers is an important part of providing excellent customer service. Here are a few tips:

  • Send a thank-you note. After a buyer has purchased your furniture, send them a thank-you note. This shows that you appreciate their business and that you are committed to providing excellent customer service.
  • Ask for feedback. A few days after the buyer has received their furniture, send them an email asking for feedback. This feedback can help you improve your products and services.
  • Offer to help. If the buyer has any questions or problems with their furniture, offer to help. This shows that you are committed to providing excellent customer service and that you want to make sure the buyer is satisfied with their purchase.
  • Follow up on a regular basis. Every few months, send the buyer an email to check in and see how they are doing. This shows that you care about your customers and that you are committed to providing excellent customer service.

By following up with buyers, you can increase your chances of building a loyal customer base that will be more likely to buy from you again in the future.
