Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your specific needs and preferences. It is also lightweight and easy to move around. * **Wicker:** Wicker is a type of woven material that is made from natural fibers, such as rattan, bamboo, or willow. Wicker furniture is lightweight and easy to care for, but it is not as durable as teak or rattan. * **Aluminum:** Aluminum is a lightweight and durable metal that is resistant to rust and corrosion. Aluminum furniture is easy to care for and maintain, and it is also relatively affordable. * **Plastic:** Plastic is a lightweight and affordable material that is available in a wide range of colors and styles.
