FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? Look no further! Here are answers to some frequently asked questions to help you make informed decisions and enjoy your outdoor space to the fullest.

Question 1: What are the different types of garden furniture materials?
Answer 1: Garden furniture comes in a variety of materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker. Wood offers a classic and natural look, while metal is durable and weather-resistant. Plastic is lightweight and low-maintenance, and wicker adds a touch of bohemian charm.

Question 2: How do I choose garden furniture that suits my needs?
Answer 2: Consider the size of your outdoor space, the intended use of the furniture, and your personal style preferences. Choose pieces that are proportional to the space and that can accommodate your desired activities, whether it's dining, lounging, or entertaining.

Question 3: What are some tips for maintaining garden furniture?
Answer 3: Regular cleaning and maintenance will help extend the lifespan of your garden furniture. Clean it with a mild detergent and water, and apply protective coatings or sealants as recommended by the manufacturer.

Question 4: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer 4: Add comfortable cushions and pillows to your seating furniture. You can also incorporate outdoor rugs and throws to create a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

Question 5: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 5: Invest in weather-resistant furniture or use protective covers when not in use. Store cushions and pillows indoors during inclement weather to prevent damage.

Question 6: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture?
Answer 6: Harvey's Furniture Stores offer a wide selection of high-quality garden furniture in various styles and materials. Their knowledgeable staff can assist you in choosing the perfect pieces for your outdoor space.

With these tips and answers to common questions, you can choose and maintain garden furniture that will enhance your outdoor living experience for years to come.
