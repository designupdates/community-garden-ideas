FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to help you make informed decisions when choosing and caring for your outdoor furniture.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer: The best materials for garden furniture depend on your specific needs and preferences. Some popular options include:

  • Wood: Classic and durable, wood furniture adds warmth and natural beauty to your garden. Choose hardwoods like teak or mahogany for exceptional durability.
  • Metal: Metal furniture, such as aluminum or wrought iron, is known for its strength and low maintenance. It's also available in various styles, from modern to traditional.
  • Plastic: Plastic furniture is lightweight, affordable, and easy to clean. It's a great choice for casual outdoor settings.
  • Rattan and Wicker: Rattan and wicker furniture bring a touch of coastal charm to your garden. These natural materials are durable and can withstand outdoor elements.

Question 2: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: To protect your garden furniture from the elements:

  • Use protective covers: When not in use, cover your furniture with waterproof covers to shield it from rain, sun, and dust.
  • Store furniture indoors: If possible, store your furniture indoors during harsh weather conditions, such as extreme heat, cold, or storms.
  • Clean regularly: Regularly clean your furniture with mild soap and water to remove dirt and debris. Avoid harsh chemicals that may damage the finish.
