FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about moving garden furniture:

Question 1: What is the best way to move garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best way to move garden furniture is to use a box van. Box vans are large, enclosed vans that are perfect for moving large and bulky items. They are also strong and durable, so they can handle the weight of heavy garden furniture.

Question 2: How do I prepare my garden furniture for moving?
Answer 2: Before you move your garden furniture, you should clean it and remove any cushions or other loose items. You should also disassemble any furniture that can be disassembled, such as tables and chairs.

Question 3: How do I load garden furniture into a van?
Answer 3: When you load garden furniture into a van, you should start with the heaviest pieces. Place the heaviest pieces in the back of the van and work your way forward. You should also secure the furniture with straps or bungee cords to prevent it from moving around during transport.

Question 4: How do I unload garden furniture from a van?
Answer 4: When you unload garden furniture from a van, you should start with the lightest pieces. Place the lightest pieces in the front of the van and work your way back. You should also be careful not to damage the furniture when you unload it.

Question 5: What should I do with my garden furniture after I move?
Answer 5: After you move, you should inspect your garden furniture for any damage. If there is any damage, you should repair it before using the furniture. You should also clean the furniture and apply a sealant to protect it from the elements.

Question 6: How can I store my garden furniture when I'm not using it?
Answer 6: When you're not using your garden furniture, you should store it in a dry place. You can store the furniture in a garage, shed, or basement. You should also cover the furniture with a tarp or plastic sheet to protect it from dust and moisture.
