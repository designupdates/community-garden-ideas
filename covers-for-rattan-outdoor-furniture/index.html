Choosing the Right Covers for Rattan Outdoor Furniture

Written by Luffy Sep 24, 2029 Â· 16 min read

Rattan outdoor furniture is a popular choice for homeowners due to its durability and stylish appearance. However, exposure to the elements can cause rattan furniture to deteriorate over time. Using covers to protect your rattan outdoor furniture from the sun, rain, and wind can help to extend its lifespan and maintain its beauty.

There are a few things to keep in mind when choosing covers for rattan outdoor furniture. First, consider the size and shape of your furniture. You will need to choose covers that are large enough to completely cover your furniture, but not so large that they are difficult to put on and take off. Second, think about the material of the covers. There are a variety of materials available, including polyester, vinyl, and canvas. Each material has its own advantages and disadvantages, so you should choose the material that is best suited for your needs.

In the next section, we will discuss the different types of covers available for rattan outdoor furniture, as well as the pros and cons of each type. We will also provide some tips for choosing the right covers for your furniture.

Covers for Rattan Outdoor Furniture

Protect your rattan furniture from the elements.

  • Measure furniture before purchasing covers.
  • Choose covers made from durable materials.
  • Consider covers with UV protection.
  • Select covers that are easy to put on and take off.
  • Store covers in a dry place when not in use.
  • Clean covers regularly to prevent mold and mildew.
  • Replace covers when they show signs of wear or damage.
  • Use a furniture cover support system for added protection.
  • Consider custom covers for unique furniture shapes.

By following these tips, you can help to protect your rattan outdoor furniture and keep it looking its best for years to come.

Measure furniture before purchasing covers.

One of the most important things to do when choosing covers for rattan outdoor furniture is to measure your furniture before you purchase covers. This will ensure that you get covers that fit properly and provide adequate protection.

To measure your furniture, you will need a tape measure. Start by measuring the length of your furniture. Then, measure the width and height. Be sure to measure the tallest and widest points of your furniture.

Once you have the measurements of your furniture, you can start shopping for covers. When you are looking at covers, be sure to check the dimensions to make sure that they are large enough to fit your furniture.

It is also a good idea to add a few inches to the measurements of your furniture when you are choosing covers. This will give you some extra room to work with when you are putting the covers on and taking them off.

By following these tips, you can be sure that you choose covers that fit your rattan outdoor furniture properly and provide adequate protection from the elements.

Choose covers made from durable materials.

Another important factor to consider when choosing covers for rattan outdoor furniture is the material of the covers. You want to choose covers that are made from durable materials that can withstand the elements.

Some of the most common materials used for outdoor furniture covers include polyester, vinyl, and canvas. Polyester is a synthetic fabric that is lightweight and water-resistant. Vinyl is a PVC-coated fabric that is waterproof and UV-resistant. Canvas is a heavy-duty cotton fabric that is durable and breathable.

When choosing a material for your outdoor furniture covers, you should consider the climate in which you live. If you live in an area with a lot of sun, you will want to choose covers that are UV-resistant. If you live in an area with a lot of rain, you will want to choose covers that are waterproof.

You should also consider the weight of the material. Heavier materials, such as canvas, are more durable, but they can also be more difficult to put on and take off. Lighter materials, such as polyester, are easier to handle, but they may not be as durable.

By choosing covers that are made from durable materials, you can help to protect your rattan outdoor furniture from the elements and extend its lifespan.

Consider covers with UV protection.

If you live in an area with a lot of sun, you should consider choosing covers for your rattan outdoor furniture that have UV protection.

  • Protects furniture from fading: UV rays from the sun can cause the color of your rattan furniture to fade over time. Covers with UV protection can help to prevent this fading and keep your furniture looking its best.
  • Prevents damage to the material: UV rays can also damage the material of your rattan furniture, making it more brittle and prone to cracking. Covers with UV protection can help to protect the material of your furniture and extend its lifespan.
  • Keeps your furniture cooler: UV rays can also heat up your rattan furniture, making it uncomfortable to sit on. Covers with UV protection can help to reflect UV rays and keep your furniture cooler.
  • Available in a variety of materials and styles: Covers with UV protection are available in a variety of materials and styles, so you can find covers that match your furniture and your personal taste.

By choosing covers with UV protection, you can help to protect your rattan outdoor furniture from the sun and extend its lifespan.

Select covers that are easy to put on and take off.

When choosing covers for your rattan outdoor furniture, it is important to select covers that are easy to put on and take off. This will make it easier for you to protect your furniture when it is not in use and to remove the covers when you want to use your furniture.

  • Drawstring or toggle closures: Covers with drawstring or toggle closures are easy to put on and take off. Simply pull the drawstring or toggle to tighten the cover around your furniture.
  • Velcro straps: Covers with Velcro straps are also easy to put on and take off. Simply align the Velcro straps and press them together to secure the cover.
  • Zippers: Covers with zippers are a bit more difficult to put on and take off than covers with drawstring or Velcro closures, but they provide a more secure fit.
  • Consider the size and weight of the covers: When choosing covers, consider the size and weight of the covers. You want to choose covers that are easy for you to handle.

By choosing covers that are easy to put on and take off, you can make it easier to protect your rattan outdoor furniture and keep it looking its best.

Store covers in a dry place when not in use.

When you are not using the covers for your rattan outdoor furniture, it is important to store them in a dry place. This will help to prevent the covers from becoming moldy or mildew.

  • Choose a dry, well-ventilated space: Choose a dry, well-ventilated space to store your covers. A garage, shed, or attic is a good option.
  • Fold or roll the covers neatly: Before you store the covers, fold or roll them neatly. This will help to prevent them from becoming wrinkled or creased.
  • Use a storage bag: You can also use a storage bag to store your covers. This will help to keep them clean and organized.
  • Check the covers regularly: Check the covers regularly for signs of mold or mildew. If you find any mold or mildew, clean the covers immediately.

By following these tips, you can help to keep your covers clean and dry and extend their lifespan.

Clean covers regularly to prevent mold and mildew.

Mold and mildew can grow on outdoor furniture covers, especially if the covers are stored in a damp or humid environment. Mold and mildew can damage the covers and make them less effective at protecting your furniture. To prevent mold and mildew, it is important to clean the covers regularly.

To clean the covers, you can use a mild detergent and water. Simply mix the detergent with water in a bucket or hose-end sprayer. Then, apply the solution to the covers and scrub them with a soft brush. Rinse the covers thoroughly with water until all of the soap is gone.

If the covers are heavily soiled, you may need to use a stronger cleaner. You can use a commercial outdoor furniture cleaner or a mixture of bleach and water. Be sure to follow the directions on the cleaner label.

Once you have cleaned the covers, allow them to dry completely before storing them. You can dry the covers in the sun or in a dryer on a low heat setting.

By cleaning the covers regularly, you can help to prevent mold and mildew and extend the lifespan of the covers.

Replace covers when they show signs of wear or damage.

Even the best quality outdoor furniture covers will eventually show signs of wear or damage. This is especially true if the covers are exposed to harsh weather conditions, such as strong winds, heavy rain, or intense sunlight. When the covers show signs of wear or damage, it is important to replace them as soon as possible.

There are a few signs that indicate that it is time to replace your outdoor furniture covers. These signs include:

  • Rips or tears: Rips or tears in the covers can allow water and dirt to reach your furniture.
  • Fading: Fading of the covers can indicate that the material is breaking down and is no longer providing adequate protection from the sun.
  • Cracking: Cracking of the covers can also indicate that the material is breaking down.
  • Mold or mildew: Mold or mildew on the covers can damage the covers and make them less effective at protecting your furniture.

If you notice any of these signs, it is time to replace your outdoor furniture covers. By replacing the covers when they show signs of wear or damage, you can help to protect your furniture and extend its lifespan.

Replacing your outdoor furniture covers is a relatively easy and inexpensive task. You can find replacement covers at most home improvement stores or online retailers.

Use a furniture cover support system for added protection.

A furniture cover support system can help to protect your outdoor furniture covers from sagging and pooling water. This is especially important for large or heavy covers. A furniture cover support system can also help to keep the covers in place during high winds.

There are a few different types of furniture cover support systems available. Some of the most popular types include:

  • Furniture cover poles: Furniture cover poles are metal or plastic poles that are inserted into the ground and then attached to the covers. This type of support system is easy to install and remove.
  • Furniture cover straps: Furniture cover straps are straps that are attached to the covers and then tightened to create a taut fit. This type of support system is also easy to install and remove.
  • Furniture cover rafter systems: Furniture cover rafter systems are more permanent than furniture cover poles or straps. They are typically made of metal or wood and are attached to the structure of your home or patio. This type of support system is more expensive than other types, but it provides the most protection.

When choosing a furniture cover support system, consider the size and weight of your covers, as well as the climate in which you live. If you live in an area with high winds, you may want to choose a more permanent support system, such as a furniture cover rafter system.

By using a furniture cover support system, you can help to protect your outdoor furniture covers and keep your furniture looking its best.

Consider custom covers for unique furniture shapes.

If you have outdoor furniture with a unique shape, you may need to consider custom covers. Custom covers are made to fit the exact dimensions of your furniture, ensuring a snug fit and maximum protection. Custom covers are also available in a wider variety of materials and styles than standard covers, so you can find covers that match your personal taste and the style of your furniture.

There are a few things to keep in mind when ordering custom covers for your outdoor furniture:

  • Provide accurate measurements: When ordering custom covers, it is important to provide accurate measurements of your furniture. This includes the length, width, and height of each piece of furniture.
  • Consider the material: Custom covers are available in a variety of materials, including polyester, vinyl, and canvas. Consider the climate in which you live and the amount of protection you need when choosing a material.
  • Choose a style: Custom covers are available in a variety of styles, including solid colors, patterns, and even personalized designs. Choose a style that matches your personal taste and the style of your furniture.

Ordering custom covers for your outdoor furniture is a great way to ensure that your furniture is protected from the elements and that your covers fit perfectly.

Custom covers may be more expensive than standard covers, but they are worth the investment if you have unique furniture shapes or if you want covers that are made from high-quality materials and that match your personal style.
