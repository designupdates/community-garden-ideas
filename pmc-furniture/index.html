Question 2: How do I clean garden furniture?
Answer: The cleaning method for garden furniture depends on the material. For example, teak furniture can be cleaned with a teak cleaner, while aluminum furniture can be cleaned with a mild soap and water solution.

Tips

Here are four practical tips to help you choose and care for your garden furniture:

Tip 1: Choose the right materials. Consider the climate in your area and the amount of maintenance you are willing to do. Some materials, such as teak, are naturally weather-resistant, while others, such as metal, may require more maintenance.

Tip 2: Measure your space. Before you purchase garden furniture, measure the space where you plan to put it. This will help you choose furniture that is the right size and scale for your outdoor space.

Tip 3: Consider your needs. Think about how you will be using your garden furniture. If you plan to entertain guests, you may want to choose a larger set with comfortable seating. If you simply want a place to relax, a smaller set or a single lounge chair may be sufficient.

Tip 4: Clean your furniture regularly. To keep your garden furniture looking its best, clean it regularly. The cleaning method will depend on the material of your furniture. For example, teak furniture can be cleaned with a teak cleaner, while metal furniture can be cleaned with a mild soap and water solution.
