FAQ

Have questions about using furniture decals on garden furniture? Here are some of the most frequently asked questions:

Question 1: Can I use furniture decals on outdoor furniture?
Yes, our furniture decals are weather-resistant and UV-resistant, so they can be used on both indoor and outdoor furniture.

Question 2: How do I apply furniture decals to garden furniture?
Applying furniture decals to garden furniture is easy. Simply clean the surface of the furniture, peel off the backing of the decal, and apply the decal to the furniture. Smooth out the decal with a credit card or squeegee to remove any air bubbles.

Question 3: How do I remove furniture decals from garden furniture?
To remove furniture decals from garden furniture, simply peel them off slowly and carefully. The decals will come off cleanly, leaving no residue behind.

Question 4: Can I use furniture decals on metal garden furniture?
Yes, our furniture decals can be used on metal garden furniture. However, it is important to clean the surface of the metal furniture thoroughly before applying the decals.

Question 5: Can I use furniture decals on plastic garden furniture?
Yes, our furniture decals can be used on plastic garden furniture. However, it is important to note that the decals may not adhere as well to plastic as they do to other materials.

Question 6: Can I use furniture decals on wooden garden furniture?
Yes, our furniture decals can be used on wooden garden furniture. However, it is important to seal the decals with a clear sealant to protect them from the elements.

Question 7: Can I reuse furniture decals?
No, our furniture decals are not reusable. Once they have been applied to a surface, they cannot be removed and reapplied.

Tips

Here are a few tips for using furniture decals on garden furniture:

Tip 1: Clean the surface of the furniture before applying the decals. This will help the decals to stick better and last longer.

Tip 2: Apply the decals in a warm, dry environment. This will help the decals to set properly.

Tip 4: Allow the decals to dry completely before using the furniture. This will help the decals to set properly and bond to the surface of the furniture.
