Moving furniture can be a daunting task, but using the right sliders can make it much easier. Sliders are thin, flat pieces of plastic or felt that are placed under the feet of furniture to make it easier to slide across floors.

There are a few different types of sliders available, so it's important to choose the right ones for your needs. Felt sliders are a good choice for hard floors, as they will protect the floor from scratches. Plastic sliders are more durable and can be used on any type of flooring. They are also available in a variety of sizes and shapes to fit different pieces of furniture.

Once you have chosen the right sliders, it's important to use them correctly. First, clean the floor where you will be moving the furniture. This will remove any dirt or debris that could damage the sliders or the floor.

Next, place the sliders under the feet of the furniture. Make sure that the sliders are centered under the feet and that they are not touching each other.

Once the sliders are in place, you can start moving the furniture. Slide the furniture slowly and carefully to avoid damaging the sliders or the floor.
