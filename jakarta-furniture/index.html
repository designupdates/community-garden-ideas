Traditional Indonesian motifs

Traditional Indonesian motifs are a key feature of Jakarta furniture. These motifs are often inspired by nature, and feature intricate patterns and designs. Some of the most common motifs include:

  • Batik: Batik is a traditional Indonesian fabric dyeing technique that uses wax to create intricate patterns. Batik motifs are often used on furniture, and can add a touch of elegance and sophistication to any piece.
  • Wayang: Wayang is a traditional Indonesian shadow puppet theater. Wayang motifs are often used on furniture, and can depict scenes from the Ramayana or Mahabharata epics.
  • Floral motifs: Floral motifs are also common in Jakarta furniture. These motifs can be simple or intricate, and can add a touch of beauty and charm to any piece.
  • Geometric motifs: Geometric motifs are another popular choice for Jakarta furniture. These motifs can be simple or complex, and can add a touch of modernity to any piece.

Traditional Indonesian motifs can be found on a wide range of Jakarta furniture, from traditional pieces to modern and contemporary designs. Whether you are looking for a piece that is inspired by the past or something more modern, you are sure to find something to your taste in Jakarta.
