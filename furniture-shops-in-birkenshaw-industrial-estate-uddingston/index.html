FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got answers. Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture to help you make informed choices for your outdoor space:

Question 1: What types of garden furniture are available?
Answer: Garden furniture comes in a wide variety of styles, materials, and designs. You can find classic wooden benches, modern metal chairs, comfortable lounge sets, and more. Choose the type of furniture that best suits your needs and the overall aesthetic of your garden.

Question 2: What materials are commonly used for garden furniture?
Answer: Garden furniture is typically made from materials that can withstand outdoor conditions, such as wood, metal, plastic, and rattan. Wood offers a classic and natural look, while metal is durable and low-maintenance. Plastic is a budget-friendly option that comes in a variety of colors and styles. Rattan is known for its lightweight and weather-resistant properties.

Question 3: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: Consider the size of your garden and the number of people you typically entertain when choosing garden furniture. Make sure to measure your space accurately to ensure that the furniture fits comfortably without overcrowding the area.

Question 4: What are some tips for maintaining garden furniture?
Answer: Regular cleaning and maintenance will help extend the lifespan of your garden furniture. Clean your furniture with a mild detergent and water, and dry it thoroughly. Apply protective sealants or oils to wooden furniture to prevent cracking and fading. Store cushions and other soft furnishings indoors during inclement weather.

Question 5: Can I leave garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: It depends on the material and construction of your garden furniture. Some materials, such as metal and plastic, can withstand outdoor conditions year-round. However, wooden furniture may require additional protection during harsh weather, such as covering it with a waterproof tarp or storing it indoors.

Question 6: Where can I find quality garden furniture?
Answer: Birkenshaw Industrial Estate Uddingston is home to a variety of furniture shops that offer a wide selection of high-quality garden furniture. Visit the furniture shops in the area to browse their collections and find pieces that suit your style and needs.
