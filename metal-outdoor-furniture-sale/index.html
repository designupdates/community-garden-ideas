Wood is a classic choice that is both durable and stylish. Metal is another durable option that is also weather-resistant. Plastic is a more affordable option that is also lightweight and easy to maintain.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, you need to consider the size of your space and the number of people you typically entertain. You also need to think about how you plan to use the furniture. If you plan to use it for dining, you will need to choose a table and chairs that are the right size for your needs. If you plan to use it for relaxing, you may want to choose a more comfortable option, such as a lounge chair or sofa.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to follow the manufacturer's instructions. However, some general tips include cleaning the furniture regularly with a mild soap and water solution, and protecting it from the elements when not in use.

Question 5: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?

Question 6: How can I update my garden furniture?
