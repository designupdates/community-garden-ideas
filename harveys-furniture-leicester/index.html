FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to help you make informed decisions and create your dream outdoor space.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The choice of material for garden furniture depends on your preferences and needs. Popular options include teak, which is naturally weather-resistant and durable; metal, which is sturdy and easy to maintain; and wicker, which adds a touch of elegance to your outdoor space.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer 2: Consider the available space in your garden and the number of people you usually entertain. Choose furniture that is proportionate to the size of your outdoor area to avoid overcrowding or making it appear cramped.

Question 3: What styles of garden furniture are available?
Answer 3: Garden furniture comes in a wide range of styles, from classic and traditional to contemporary and modern. Choose a style that complements your home's architecture and your personal taste to create a cohesive look.

Question 4: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 4: To prolong the life of your garden furniture, consider using protective covers when not in use. You can also apply weather-resistant treatments or stains to protect against sun damage and moisture.

Question 5: How do I care for and maintain my garden furniture?
Answer 5: Regular cleaning and maintenance are essential to keep your garden furniture looking its best. Use mild soap and water to clean the furniture, and avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials. For wooden furniture, apply oil or sealant periodically to protect the finish.

Question 6: Where can I find quality garden furniture?
Answer 6: Harvey's Furniture Leicester offers a wide selection of high-quality garden furniture to suit every taste and budget. Visit our showroom or browse our online catalog to find the perfect pieces for your outdoor space.

With these tips and answers to frequently asked questions, you can confidently choose and care for garden furniture that enhances your outdoor living experience.

Tips

Transform your outdoor space into an inviting oasis with these practical tips for selecting and caring for garden furniture:

Tip 1: Choose comfortable and durable furniture:
Opt for garden furniture that provides both comfort and durability. Consider factors such as the ergonomics of the seating, the quality of the materials, and the overall construction to ensure your furniture can withstand outdoor conditions and provide years of enjoyment.

Tip 2: Consider your outdoor space and needs:
Take into account the size and layout of your garden when choosing furniture. Select pieces that are proportionate to the available space to avoid overcrowding or making it appear cramped. Think about the primary use of your outdoor area, whether it's relaxation, dining, or entertaining, to choose furniture that suits your needs.

Tip 3: Create a cohesive design:
Choose garden furniture that complements the style of your home and garden. Consider the colors, textures, and materials of your existing décor to create a harmonious look. Adding cushions, throws, and other accessories can also help personalize your outdoor space and make it more inviting.

Tip 4: Maintain your garden furniture regularly:
Regular cleaning and maintenance are essential to keep your garden furniture looking its best and prolong its lifespan. Use mild soap and water to clean the furniture, and avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials. For wooden furniture, apply oil or sealant periodically to protect the finish. Additionally, consider using protective covers when the furniture is not in use to shield it from harsh weather conditions.

By following these tips, you can create a stylish and functional outdoor space that invites relaxation and enjoyment for years to come.
