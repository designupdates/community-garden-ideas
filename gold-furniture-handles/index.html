Easy to install

Gold furniture handles are relatively easy to install, and most people can do it themselves with a few simple tools. Here are the steps on how to install gold furniture handles:

  1. Remove the old hardware. Start by removing the old hardware from your cabinets or drawers. This can usually be done by unscrewing the screws that are holding the hardware in place.
  2. Measure the distance between the screw holes. Once you have removed the old hardware, measure the distance between the screw holes. This will help you determine the size of the new handles you need.
  3. Mark the location of the new screw holes. Use a pencil to mark the location of the new screw holes on the cabinet or drawer. Make sure that the holes are evenly spaced and that they are centered on the drawer or cabinet front.
  4. Drill pilot holes. Drill pilot holes at the marks you made in the previous step. This will help to prevent the wood from splitting when you screw in the new handles.
  5. Screw in the new handles. Align the new handles with the pilot holes and screw them into place. Be sure to tighten the screws securely, but do not overtighten them.
Here are some tips for installing gold furniture handles:
  • Use a drill bit that is the same size as the screws that came with the handles.
  • Be careful not to overtighten the screws, as this could damage the handles or the wood.
  • If you are installing handles on a drawer, make sure that the handles are centered on the drawer front.
  • If you are installing handles on a cabinet, make sure that the handles are evenly spaced and that they are centered on the cabinet door.
Installing gold furniture handles is a quick and easy way to update the look of your home decor. With a few simple tools and a little bit of time, you can give your furniture a fresh new look.
