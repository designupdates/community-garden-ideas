Question 2: How do I choose the right garden furniture for my space?
Answer 2: When choosing garden furniture, consider the size of your outdoor space, your personal style, and the intended use. Measure your space to ensure the furniture fits comfortably, and choose pieces that complement your home's exterior and garden design.

Question 3: What materials are commonly used for garden furniture?
Answer 3: Common materials for garden furniture include wood, metal, plastic, and rattan. Wood offers a classic and natural look, metal is durable and weather-resistant, plastic is affordable and low-maintenance, and rattan adds a touch of bohemian charm.

Answer 4: To protect your garden furniture from sun, rain, and wind, consider using furniture covers, cushions, and pillows. You can also apply protective coatings or sealants to wooden furniture to enhance its durability.

Answer 5: Regular cleaning and maintenance will help extend the life of your garden furniture. Use mild soap and water to clean the furniture, and dry it thoroughly to prevent rust or mold. For wooden furniture, apply a sealant or oil periodically to protect the wood.
