Have questions about garden furniture? Look no further! We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to help you make informed decisions about your outdoor space.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?

Answer 1: The choice of material for garden furniture depends on your preferences and needs. Popular options include:

  • Wood: Classic and durable, but requires regular maintenance.
  • Metal: Strong and weather-resistant, but can be prone to rust.
  • Plastic: Lightweight and low-maintenance, but may not be as durable.
  • Rattan: Natural-looking and eco-friendly, but can be more expensive.

Question 2: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?

Answer 2: To protect your garden furniture from harsh weather conditions:

  • Use weather-resistant covers: When not in use, cover your furniture with waterproof covers.
  • Store furniture indoors during extreme weather: If possible, move your furniture to a sheltered area during storms or heavy rain.
  • Apply protective coatings: Regularly apply protective coatings or sealants to wooden furniture to prevent moisture damage.
