FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? Here are some frequently asked questions and answers to help you make informed decisions.

Question 1: What materials are used to make garden furniture?
Answer: Garden furniture is typically made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker. Each material has its own unique advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to choose the one that best suits your needs and preferences.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing garden furniture, it's important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You'll also want to think about the style of furniture you prefer and how it will complement your outdoor decor.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You should also apply a sealant or protectant to the furniture to help protect it from the elements.

Question 4: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: Whether or not you can leave your garden furniture outside all year round depends on the material it is made from. Some materials, such as teak and aluminum, are more weather-resistant than others. If you do choose to leave your furniture outside, be sure to cover it with a tarp or furniture covers during inclement weather.

Question 5: How do I assemble my garden furniture?
Answer: Most garden furniture comes with easy-to-follow assembly instructions. Be sure to read the instructions carefully before you begin assembling the furniture. If you have any questions, you can always contact the manufacturer for assistance.

Question 6: Where can I find garden furniture sales?
Answer: Many retailers offer sales on garden furniture throughout the year. You can find sales at both online and brick-and-mortar stores. Be sure to compare prices from different retailers before making a purchase.
