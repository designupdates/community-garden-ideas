Affordable Cat Furniture: A Comprehensive Guide to Finding the Best Deals

Written by Adams Sep 17, 2026 · 15 min read

Cats are beloved companions, but their furniture can often come with a hefty price tag. Luckily, finding affordable cat furniture doesn't have to be a challenge. With a bit of research and clever shopping, you can create a comfortable and stylish haven for your feline friend without breaking the bank.

This comprehensive guide will help you navigate the world of affordable cat furniture. We'll provide insider tips, money-saving strategies, and product recommendations to empower you with the knowledge you need to find the perfect pieces for your cat's needs and budget.

Before diving into the details, let's explore the types of cat furniture available and their benefits:

affordable cat furniture

When searching for affordable cat furniture, consider these key points:

  • Shop online for wider selection
  • Compare prices from multiple retailers
  • Look for sales and discounts
  • Consider DIY projects for customization
  • Repurpose old furniture
  • Buy secondhand from thrift stores
  • Make use of cardboard boxes
  • Utilize vertical space with cat trees
  • Prioritize functionality over aesthetics
  • Choose durable materials for longevity

By following these tips, you can find affordable cat furniture that meets your budget and your cat's needs.

Shop online for wider selection

Online retailers offer a vast selection of cat furniture at various price points, allowing you to compare and choose the best option for your budget. Websites like Amazon, Chewy, and Wayfair feature a wide range of cat trees, scratching posts, beds, and other accessories.

Shopping online also gives you access to exclusive deals and discounts that may not be available in physical stores. Many online retailers offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount, further reducing the cost of your purchase.

When shopping online, be sure to read reviews from other customers to get an idea of the quality and durability of the furniture. You can also filter search results by price to narrow down your options and find the most affordable choices.

Once you've found a few pieces you like, compare prices from multiple retailers to make sure you're getting the best deal. Some websites, like Google Shopping, allow you to compare prices from different retailers side-by-side.

By shopping online for your cat furniture, you can save money and find a wider selection of products to choose from. Just be sure to do your research and compare prices before making a purchase.

Compare prices from multiple retailers

Once you've found a few pieces of cat furniture that you like, the next step is to compare prices from multiple retailers. This will help you ensure that you're getting the best deal possible.

There are a few different ways to compare prices. One option is to visit each retailer's website and compare the prices of the same item. Another option is to use a price comparison website, such as Google Shopping or PriceGrabber. These websites allow you to enter the name of the product you're looking for and compare prices from multiple retailers side-by-side.

When comparing prices, be sure to factor in the cost of shipping. Some retailers offer free shipping, while others charge a flat rate or a percentage of the total order amount. If you're ordering multiple items, it's worth checking to see if any of the retailers offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount.

Once you've compared prices and factored in the cost of shipping, you can choose the retailer that offers the best deal. By taking the time to compare prices, you can save money on your cat furniture purchase.

Repurpose old furniture

You can also turn an old ottoman into a cat bed by adding a cushion and some soft fabric. And you can even turn an old cardboard box into a scratching post by covering it with carpet or sisal rope.

Buy secondhand from thrift stores

Thrift stores are a great place to find affordable cat furniture. You can often find gently used cat trees, scratching posts, and beds for a fraction of the cost of new furniture.

  • Wide selection: Thrift stores often have a wide selection of cat furniture to choose from, so you're sure to find something that you like.
  • Affordable prices: Thrift stores sell used furniture at a fraction of the cost of new furniture, so you can save a lot of money.
  • Unique finds: Thrift stores often have unique and one-of-a-kind items, so you can find something special for your cat.
  • Support a good cause: When you buy from a thrift store, you're supporting a good cause. Thrift stores often donate their profits to charities.

Here are a few tips for buying secondhand cat furniture from thrift stores:

  • Inspect the furniture carefully before you buy it. Make sure that it is sturdy and well-made.
  • Clean the furniture thoroughly before you bring it home. This will help to remove any dirt, dust, or allergens.
  • Be patient. It may take some time to find the perfect piece of cat furniture at a thrift store. But if you're patient, you're sure to find something great.

Make use of cardboard boxes

Cardboard boxes are a great way to provide your cat with affordable and fun furniture. Cats love to play in and hide in boxes, so you can easily turn a few cardboard boxes into a cat castle or playhouse.

To make a cardboard box cat castle, simply cut out a few holes in the sides of the boxes and stack them on top of each other. You can also add some tunnels or ramps to connect the boxes.

To make a cardboard box cat playhouse, cut out a few windows and a door in the sides of the box. You can also add some toys or treats inside the box to make it more fun for your cat.

Cardboard boxes are also great for making scratching posts. Simply cover a cardboard box with carpet or sisal rope and your cat will have a place to scratch that won't damage your furniture.

Here are a few tips for using cardboard boxes for cat furniture:

  • Choose sturdy cardboard boxes that are large enough for your cat to fit in.
  • Cut out the holes and windows carefully to avoid sharp edges.
  • Add some toys or treats to the boxes to make them more fun for your cat.
  • Supervise your cat when they are playing with cardboard boxes, especially if they are kittens.

Utilize vertical space with cat trees

Cat trees are a great way to provide your cat with a place to climb, scratch, and play without taking up too much floor space. Cat trees come in a variety of sizes and styles, so you can find one that fits your home and your cat's needs.

  • Save space: Cat trees are a great way to save space in your home. They can be placed in a corner or against a wall, so they won't take up much floor space.
  • Provide exercise: Cat trees give your cat a place to climb and play, which is important for their physical and mental health.
  • Satisfy scratching needs: Cat trees often have scratching posts built in, so your cat can satisfy their natural scratching needs without damaging your furniture.
  • Provide a safe place to hide: Cat trees can provide your cat with a safe place to hide and sleep, which is especially important for shy or anxious cats.

Here are a few tips for choosing a cat tree:

  • Choose a cat tree that is the right size for your cat and your home.
  • Look for a cat tree that has a variety of features, such as scratching posts, perches, and hiding places.
  • Choose a cat tree that is made from durable materials that will last for years.

Prioritize functionality over aesthetics

When choosing cat furniture, it's important to prioritize functionality over aesthetics. This means choosing furniture that is comfortable, safe, and durable, even if it's not the most stylish piece in the room.

  • Comfort: Your cat's comfort should be your top priority when choosing cat furniture. Choose furniture that is soft, supportive, and provides a comfortable place for your cat to sleep and relax.
  • Safety: Cat furniture should be safe for your cat to use. Avoid furniture with sharp edges or small parts that could be a choking hazard. Choose furniture that is sturdy and well-made, so it won't collapse or tip over.
  • Durability: Cat furniture should be durable enough to withstand your cat's claws and teeth. Choose furniture that is made from high-quality materials that will last for years.
  • Easy to clean: Cat furniture should be easy to clean. Choose furniture that can be wiped down with a damp cloth or machine-washed.

By prioritizing functionality over aesthetics, you can choose cat furniture that will provide your cat with years of comfort and enjoyment.

Choose durable materials for longevity

When choosing cat furniture, it's important to choose furniture that is made from durable materials that will last for years. This will save you money in the long run, as you won't have to replace the furniture as often.

  • Wood: Wood is a durable material that is often used to make cat furniture. Wood furniture is sturdy and can withstand your cat's claws and teeth. It is also easy to clean and maintain.
  • Metal: Metal is another durable material that is often used to make cat furniture. Metal furniture is strong and sturdy, and it is also easy to clean. However, metal furniture can be more expensive than wood furniture.
  • Plastic: Plastic is a less durable material than wood or metal, but it is also less expensive. Plastic furniture is lightweight and easy to clean, but it can be scratched or damaged by your cat's claws.
  • Fabric: Fabric is a soft and comfortable material that is often used to make cat beds and cushions. However, fabric can be easily scratched or torn by your cat's claws. It is also important to choose a fabric that is easy to clean.

By choosing cat furniture that is made from durable materials, you can ensure that your cat will have a comfortable and safe place to sleep and play for years to come.
