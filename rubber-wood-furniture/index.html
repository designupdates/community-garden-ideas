Written by William Mar 08, 2029 · 17 min read

Rubber wood furniture has become increasingly popular in recent years due to its unique combination of sustainability, durability, and affordability. If you're looking for a stylish and eco-conscious furniture option, you might consider rubber wood for your next purchase. Here's everything you need to know about this remarkable material:

Rubber wood is harvested from rubber trees that no longer produce latex for commercial purposes. These trees are typically around 25 years old when they are felled, after which they are processed into lumber. The wood from these trees is strong, durable, and has a rich, golden-brown color. It is also naturally resistant to pests and fungi, making it an ideal choice for furniture that will be used in humid or tropical climates.

In addition to its practical benefits, rubber wood is also a sustainable choice. Rubber trees are grown in plantations, which means that they can be replanted after they have been harvested. This makes rubber wood a renewable resource, which is important for preserving our planet's forests.
