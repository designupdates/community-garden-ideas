Durable and weather-resistant

Rattan patio furniture is renowned for its exceptional durability and weather resistance. This makes it an ideal choice for outdoor use, as it can withstand the elements and maintain its beauty for years to come.

  • Sturdy construction: Rattan patio furniture is typically made with sturdy frames of aluminum, steel, or wicker. These frames provide excellent support and ensure that the furniture can withstand regular use.
  • Weather-resistant materials: Natural rattan is naturally resistant to moisture and UV rays, making it suitable for outdoor use. Synthetic rattan is also weather-resistant and designed to withstand fading, cracking, and peeling.
  • Low maintenance: Rattan patio furniture is easy to maintain. Simply clean it with a damp cloth and mild soap solution as needed. Unlike wood or metal furniture, rattan does not require regular painting or staining.
