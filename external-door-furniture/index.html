External Door Furniture Buying Guide

External door furniture is an important part of any home's security and curb appeal. It can make a big difference in the first impression your guests have of your home, and it can also help to protect you and your family from intruders.

When choosing external door furniture, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to consider the style of your home. You'll want to choose hardware that complements the architecture of your home and that fits in with the overall design scheme.

Complements home style

External door furniture can also play a key role in complementing the style of your home.

  • Traditional homes: For traditional homes, classic door furniture designs such as ironmongery or brass knockers can add a touch of elegance and charm.
  • Contemporary homes: Modern homes may suit more contemporary door furniture designs, such as sleek stainless steel handles or minimalist letter plates.
  • Coastal homes: Coastal homes can benefit from door furniture made from materials that are resistant to rust and corrosion, such as stainless steel or aluminium.
  • Country homes: Rustic door furniture, such as wrought iron handles or wooden door knockers, can add character and warmth to country homes.

By choosing door furniture that matches the style of your home, you can create a cohesive and inviting look.

Protects from intruders

External door furniture can also help to protect your home from intruders.

1. Strong and durable materials: High-quality door furniture is made from strong and durable materials, such as stainless steel or brass, which can withstand physical attacks and forced entry attempts.

2. Secure locking mechanisms: Deadbolts and other secure locking mechanisms can be incorporated into door handles and knobs to prevent intruders from picking or bypassing the lock.

3. Reinforcing plates: Reinforcing plates can be installed behind door handles and other vulnerable points to provide additional protection against forced entry.

4. Anti-snap features: Some door handles and cylinders are equipped with anti-snap features, which make it difficult for intruders to break or snap the lock.

By choosing external door furniture with robust security features, you can make it more difficult for intruders to gain access to your home.

Improves curb appeal

External door furniture can also play a significant role in improving the curb appeal of your home.

1. Visual appeal: Well-chosen door furniture can enhance the visual appeal of your front door and make your home more inviting.

2. Complements architectural style: Door furniture that complements the architectural style of your home can create a cohesive and stylish look.

3. Adds character: Unique or decorative door furniture can add character and personality to your home's exterior.

4. Increases perceived value: High-quality door furniture can increase the perceived value of your home, making it more attractive to potential buyers.

By choosing external door furniture that is both stylish and secure, you can make a positive impression on visitors and enhance the overall look of your home.

Durable and long-lasting

External door furniture should be durable and long-lasting to withstand the elements and daily use.

1. High-quality materials: Choose door furniture made from high-quality materials, such as stainless steel or brass, which are resistant to rust and corrosion.

2. Solid construction: Look for door furniture that is solid and well-constructed, with no weak points or flimsy parts.

3. Protective finishes: Some door furniture is treated with protective finishes, such as powder coating or galvanisation, to enhance durability and resistance to wear and tear.

4. Regular maintenance: Regular maintenance, such as cleaning and lubricating moving parts, can help to extend the lifespan of your door furniture.

By choosing durable and long-lasting external door furniture, you can avoid the need for frequent repairs or replacements, saving you time and money in the long run.
