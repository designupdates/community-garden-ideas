Easy to use

Lancaster furniture cream is incredibly easy to use. It comes in a convenient pump bottle that dispenses just the right amount of cream for each application. The cream is also very easy to spread, and it absorbs quickly into the wood without leaving a greasy residue.

To use Lancaster furniture cream, simply follow these steps:
