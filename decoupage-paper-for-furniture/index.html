Easy to apply and remove.

Decoupage paper for furniture is incredibly user-friendly, making it a great choice for both beginners and experienced crafters. Applying decoupage paper is a straightforward process that can be easily mastered with a few simple steps.

  • Cut the decoupage paper to the desired size and shape.

    Use a sharp craft knife or scissors to precisely cut the decoupage paper to fit the surface you want to decorate.

  • Apply a coat of decoupage medium to the surface.

    Use a brush to apply a thin, even coat of decoupage medium to the surface of the furniture. This will help the decoupage paper adhere properly.

  • Place the decoupage paper onto the surface.

    Carefully position the decoupage paper onto the surface, ensuring that it is straight and smooth. Use your fingers or a soft cloth to gently press the decoupage paper onto the surface, removing any air bubbles.

  • Apply a top coat of decoupage medium.

    Once the decoupage paper is in place, apply a coat of decoupage medium over the entire surface. This will seal the decoupage paper and protect it from wear and tear.

Removing decoupage paper is just as easy as applying it. Simply soak the surface with warm water and gently scrape off the decoupage paper using a plastic scraper or your fingernails. Once the decoupage paper is removed, you can clean the surface and start over with a new design.

  • Can be repaired easily.

    In the unlikely event that your decoupage paper gets damaged, it can be easily repaired. Simply remove the damaged section and replace it with a new piece of decoupage paper, following the same application process.

With proper care and maintenance, decoupage paper for furniture can last for many years, allowing you to enjoy your transformed furniture pieces for generations to come.
