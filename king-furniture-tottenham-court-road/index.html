FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got answers! Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture to help you make informed decisions and create a beautiful and functional outdoor space.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer: The best materials for garden furniture depend on your individual needs and preferences. Common materials include:

  • Wood: Wood is a classic choice for garden furniture, offering natural beauty and durability. Teak, cedar, and eucalyptus are popular choices due to their resistance to rot and decay.
  • Metal: Metal furniture, such as aluminum and wrought iron, is known for its strength and durability. It is also easy to clean and maintain.
  • Plastic: Plastic furniture is a budget-friendly option that comes in a variety of colors and styles. It is lightweight and easy to move around.
  • Rattan and Wicker: Rattan and wicker furniture adds a touch of natural charm to your garden. These materials are durable and can withstand outdoor conditions.

Question 2: How can I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing garden furniture, it's important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. Measure your space carefully and choose furniture that fits comfortably without overcrowding the area.

Question 3: What features should I look for in garden furniture?
Answer: Look for furniture with features that enhance comfort and functionality. Consider adjustable chairs, reclining loungers, ottomans, and side tables with built-in storage.

Question 4: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: To protect your garden furniture from rain, sun, and other harsh weather conditions, consider using protective covers or storing the furniture indoors during extreme weather.

Question 5: How can I keep my garden furniture clean and well-maintained?
Answer: Regular cleaning and maintenance will help keep your garden furniture looking its best. Use mild soap and water to clean the furniture, and apply protective oils or sealants to wooden furniture as needed.

Question 6: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture?
Answer: For a wide selection of high-quality garden furniture, visit King Furniture Tottenham Court Road. Our experienced team can help you choose the perfect furniture for your outdoor space.

With these tips, you can choose and maintain garden furniture that will enhance your outdoor living space for years to come.
