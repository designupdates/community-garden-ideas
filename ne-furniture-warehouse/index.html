Delivery options

Many furniture warehouses offer delivery options to make it easy to get your new furniture home. This is especially helpful if you are purchasing large or heavy furniture items. Here are some of the delivery options that furniture warehouses typically offer:

Standard delivery: Standard delivery is the most basic delivery option and typically includes delivery to your home's front door. This option is usually free or low-cost.

Inside delivery: Inside delivery includes delivery to your home's inside door, such as your living room or bedroom. This option is typically more expensive than standard delivery.

White glove delivery: White glove delivery is the most premium delivery option and includes delivery to your home's room of choice, as well as unpacking and assembly of your furniture. This option is typically the most expensive, but it is also the most convenient.

When choosing a delivery option, it is important to consider the size and weight of your furniture, as well as your budget.
