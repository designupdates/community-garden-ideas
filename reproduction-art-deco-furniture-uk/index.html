How to care for reproduction Art Deco furniture

Reproduction Art Deco furniture is made with high-quality materials, but it is important to care for it properly to ensure that it lasts for many years to come. Here are a few tips on how to care for your reproduction Art Deco furniture:

  1. Dust regularly. Use a soft, dry cloth to dust your reproduction Art Deco furniture regularly. This will help to remove dust and dirt, which can damage the finish of the furniture.
  2. Clean spills immediately. If you spill something on your reproduction Art Deco furniture, clean it up immediately. Use a soft, damp cloth to wipe up the spill. Do not use harsh chemicals or cleaners, as these can damage the finish of the furniture.
  3. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight. Direct sunlight can damage the finish of your reproduction Art Deco furniture. Place your furniture in a spot where it will not be exposed to direct sunlight for long periods of time.
  4. Use coasters and placemats. Use coasters under glasses and placemats under plates to protect the surface of your reproduction Art Deco furniture from scratches and stains.
  5. Have your furniture professionally cleaned periodically. Every few years, you should have your reproduction Art Deco furniture professionally cleaned. This will help to remove dirt and grime that cannot be removed with regular dusting and cleaning.

By following these tips, you can help to ensure that your reproduction Art Deco furniture lasts for many years to come.
