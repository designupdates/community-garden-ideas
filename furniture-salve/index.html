Nourishes and conditions wood.

At the heart of furniture salve's efficacy lies its ability to nourish and condition wood, restoring its natural beauty and extending its lifespan.

  • Beeswax:

    A natural wax produced by honeybees, beeswax is a key ingredient in furniture salve. It penetrates deep into the wood, providing long-lasting nourishment and protection. Beeswax also helps to repel water and dirt, preventing damage to the wood.

  • Olive oil:

    Renowned for its moisturizing properties, olive oil helps to soften and condition wood, preventing it from drying out and cracking. Its rich antioxidants also protect the wood from damage caused by free radicals.

  • Coconut oil:

    Another nourishing oil, coconut oil is easily absorbed by wood, providing deep conditioning and protection. It also possesses antimicrobial properties, helping to prevent mold and mildew growth on wooden surfaces.

  • Lemon essential oil:

    Derived from lemon peels, lemon essential oil is a natural disinfectant and deodorizer. It helps to clean and freshen wood while leaving behind a pleasant citrus scent.

Easy to apply and remove.

Furniture salve is incredibly easy to apply and remove, making it a convenient choice for busy furniture owners.

  • Apply:

    To apply furniture salve, simply scoop out a small amount with a clean cloth or sponge. Rub the salve onto the wood in the direction of the grain. You can use a circular motion to work the salve into the wood. Continue applying the salve until the wood is evenly coated.

  • Remove:

    To remove furniture salve, simply wipe it off with a clean cloth. You can use a damp cloth if the salve is particularly thick or dirty. There is no need to rinse the wood after removing the salve.

  • Frequency:

    The frequency with which you need to apply furniture salve will depend on the type of wood and the conditions in your home. In general, it is a good idea to apply furniture salve every few months, or more often if the wood is exposed to a lot of sunlight or moisture.

  • Tips:

    For best results, apply furniture salve in a well-ventilated area. You can also wear gloves to protect your hands. If you are applying furniture salve to a large piece of furniture, you may want to work in sections.

With its easy application and removal process, furniture salve is a convenient and effective way to protect and preserve your wooden furniture.
