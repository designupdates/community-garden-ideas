Sustainable materials

Candosa garden furniture is committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly materials and manufacturing processes to minimize its environmental impact.

  • Recycled plastic

    Many Candosa products are made from recycled plastic, such as HDPE (high-density polyethylene) and PET (polyethylene terephthalate). These materials are durable, weather-resistant, and require less energy to produce than virgin plastic.

  • FSC-certified wood

    Candosa also uses FSC-certified wood, which comes from responsibly managed forests. FSC certification ensures that the wood is harvested in a sustainable manner, protecting biodiversity and ecosystems.

  • Natural materials

    Candosa incorporates natural materials such as rattan, bamboo, and teak into its designs. These materials are renewable and biodegradable, making them a sustainable choice for garden furniture.

  • Low-VOC finishes

    Candosa uses low-VOC (volatile organic compound) finishes on its furniture. VOCs are harmful air pollutants that can contribute to smog and other environmental problems. Low-VOC finishes help to reduce the environmental impact of the manufacturing process.

By using sustainable materials, Candosa garden furniture helps to reduce waste, conserve natural resources, and protect the environment.

FAQ

Have more questions about garden furniture? Here are some frequently asked questions and their answers:

Question 1: How do I choose the right garden furniture for my needs?
Answer 1: Consider factors such as the size of your outdoor space, the purpose of the furniture (dining, lounging, etc.), your personal style, and your budget. Measure your space and make a list of your needs and preferences before shopping.

Question 2: What are the different types of garden furniture materials?
Answer 2: Common materials include wood, metal (aluminum, steel, wrought iron), plastic (recycled plastic, HDPE, PVC), and natural materials (rattan, wicker, bamboo). Each material has its own unique characteristics and benefits.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 3: Regular cleaning and maintenance can extend the lifespan of your garden furniture. Follow the manufacturer's care instructions for specific materials. General tips include wiping down furniture with a damp cloth, avoiding harsh chemicals, and storing furniture indoors or under a cover during harsh weather.

Question 4: How can I create a comfortable and inviting outdoor space with my garden furniture?
Answer 4: Arrange furniture in a way that promotes conversation and relaxation. Add cushions, pillows, and throws for extra comfort. Incorporate plants and flowers to bring life and color to your outdoor space. Consider adding an outdoor rug to define the area and create a cozy atmosphere.

Question 5: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 5: Use weather-resistant furniture covers when not in use. Choose furniture made from durable materials that can withstand rain, sun, and wind. Store cushions and other accessories indoors during inclement weather.

Question 6: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture at a reasonable price?
Answer 6: Consider brands like Candosa that offer a wide range of stylish and durable garden furniture made from sustainable materials. Shop online or visit reputable furniture stores to compare prices and find the best deals.
