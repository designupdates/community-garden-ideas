Think about the finish of the furniture board

The finish of the furniture board is also an important consideration. There are many different types of finishes available, from matte to glossy. The type of finish you choose will depend on the look you want to achieve.

  • Textured finishes add a unique look to furniture. They are available in a variety of textures, from wood grain to leather.
