come in various finishes

furniture finish is the final coating applied to the surface of the furniture. It can be used to protect the furniture from wear and tear, to give it a certain look, or to match it to other pieces of furniture in a room.

  • painted finishes

    Painted finishes are one of the most common types of furniture finishes. They are available in a wide range of colors and can be used to create a variety of looks, from traditional to modern.

  • stained finishes

    Stained finishes are another popular type of furniture finish. They are created by applying a stain to the wood, which penetrates the wood and changes its color. Stained finishes can be used to create a variety of looks, from light and airy to dark and dramatic.

  • natural finishes

    Natural finishes are finishes that do not change the color of the wood. They are often used on furniture made from expensive woods, such as mahogany or walnut. Natural finishes can be used to highlight the natural beauty of the wood.

  • distressed finishes

    Distressed finishes are finishes that make furniture look old and worn. They are often used on furniture that is made to look like antiques. Distressed finishes can be created using a variety of techniques, such as sanding, scraping, and painting.

The type of finish that you choose for your furniture will depend on your personal taste and the style of your home. If you are not sure which finish to choose, you can always consult with a professional furniture refinisher.
