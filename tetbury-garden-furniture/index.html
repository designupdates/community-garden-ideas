Stainless Steel: Known for its exceptional strength and corrosion resistance, stainless steel is used in Tetbury furniture frames and accents. It ensures durability in all weather conditions, making it an ideal choice for coastal or poolside settings.

Tips

Creating a stylish and comfortable outdoor space with garden furniture is easy with these practical tips:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Size and Scale: Consider the size of your outdoor space and choose furniture that is appropriately scaled. Avoid overcrowding the area with too many pieces or using furniture that is too large or too small.

Tip 2: Mix and Match Materials: Don't be afraid to mix and match different materials in your garden furniture. For example, pair teak chairs with a stainless steel table or add some woven accents to create a unique and interesting look.

Tip 3: Add Comfortable Cushions: Make your garden furniture more inviting and comfortable by adding cushions. Choose cushions that are made from weather-resistant fabrics and that provide the desired level of support.

Tip 4: Accessorize with Plants and Textiles: Bring life and color to your outdoor space by adding plants and textiles to your garden furniture. Place potted plants around the seating area and use outdoor rugs and throws to add warmth and texture.

By following these tips, you can create a garden furniture arrangement that is both stylish and comfortable, making your outdoor space a true oasis for relaxation and enjoyment.
