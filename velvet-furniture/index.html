Requires regular maintenance and care

Velvet furniture requires regular maintenance and care to keep it looking its best and prolong its lifespan. Here are a few tips for caring for your velvet furniture:

Vacuum regularly: Vacuum your velvet furniture regularly to remove dust and dirt. Use a soft brush attachment to avoid damaging the pile. Vacuuming regularly will help prevent the accumulation of dust and dirt, which can dull the fabric and make it more difficult to clean.

Brush against the nap: When brushing your velvet furniture, always brush against the nap. Brushing with the nap will help to lift dirt and debris and prevent the pile from becoming matted. Use a soft brush with natural bristles to avoid damaging the fabric.

Avoid direct sunlight: Direct sunlight can fade velvet fabric over time. If your velvet furniture is exposed to direct sunlight, consider using curtains or blinds to protect it. You can also rotate your furniture periodically to ensure that all areas are exposed to sunlight evenly.

Spot clean spills immediately: If you spill something on your velvet furniture, blot it up immediately with a clean, white cloth. Do not rub, as this can damage the fabric. Once the spill is blotted up, you can use a mild soap and water solution to clean the area. Test the solution in an inconspicuous area first to make sure it does not damage the fabric.

By following these simple care tips, you can keep your velvet furniture looking its best for years to come.
