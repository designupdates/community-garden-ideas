Consider maintenance: Some materials require more maintenance than others.

  • Aluminum: Aluminum is a lightweight and durable metal that is resistant to rust and corrosion. However, aluminum furniture can become dirty and stained over time.   • Resin: Resin is a synthetic material that is resistant to fading, cracking, and peeling. However, resin furniture can become dirty and stained over time. To clean resin furniture, use a mild soap and water solution and a soft cloth. You can also use a commercial resin cleaner to remove stubborn stains.
  • Other materials: There are a number of other materials that can be used for Florida garden furniture, such as stainless steel, wicker, and concrete.
