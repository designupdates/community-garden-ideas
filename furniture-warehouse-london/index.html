Question 2: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer 2: The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. Wood is a classic choice that is durable and stylish, but it requires some maintenance. Metal is another durable option that is easy to care for, but it can be more expensive than wood.
