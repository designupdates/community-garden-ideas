Tips

Here are a few tips to help you keep your garden furniture in good condition:

Tip 1: Clean your garden furniture regularly.
This will help to remove dirt and grime, and prevent the build-up of mould and mildew.

Tip 2: Protect your garden furniture from the elements.
If possible, store your garden furniture indoors during the winter months. If this is not possible, cover your furniture with a waterproof cover.

Tip 3: Treat your wooden garden furniture with a wood preserver.
This will help to protect the wood from rot and decay.

Tip 4: Regularly inspect your garden furniture for signs of damage.
If you find any damage, repair it as soon as possible to prevent further deterioration.

Tip 5: Store your garden furniture in a dry place over the winter months.
This will help to prevent rust and corrosion.

By following these tips, you can help to keep your garden furniture in good condition for longer.
