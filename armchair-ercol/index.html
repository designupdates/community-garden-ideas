Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best material for garden furniture is a matter of personal preference. Some of the most popular materials include teak, cedar, redwood, and aluminum. Teak is a very durable wood that is resistant to rot and decay. Cedar and redwood are also durable woods, but they are not as resistant to rot and decay as teak. Aluminum is a lightweight and durable metal that is resistant to rust and corrosion.

Question 2: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 2: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You should also protect your furniture from the elements by covering it with a tarp or storing it in a garage or shed during the winter months.

Question 3: How long will my garden furniture last?
Answer 3: The lifespan of your garden furniture will depend on the material it is made from and how well you care for it. Teak furniture can last for 50 years or more, while cedar and redwood furniture can last for 20-30 years. Aluminum furniture can last for 10-15 years.
