Sustainable Materials

Ashford Furniture is committed to sustainability and incorporates eco-friendly materials into their designs whenever possible. This commitment stems from the company's belief that businesses have a responsibility to protect the environment and minimize their ecological footprint.

The recycled metals used by Ashford Furniture are carefully selected to ensure that they meet the company's high standards for quality and durability.

Introduction Paragraph for FAQ:

If you're considering adding garden furniture to your outdoor space, you may have some questions. Here are answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?

Answer 1: The best materials for garden furniture depend on your individual needs and preferences. Some popular options include:

  • Wood: Wood is a classic choice for garden furniture, offering warmth and natural beauty. Teak and cedar are popular choices due to their durability and resistance to rot.
  • Metal: Metal furniture is durable and easy to maintain. Aluminum and wrought iron are common choices, as they are resistant to rust and corrosion.
  • Plastic: Plastic furniture is lightweight, affordable, and easy to clean. It is also available in a wide range of colors and styles.
  • Wicker: Wicker furniture is made from natural materials such as rattan or bamboo. It is lightweight and durable, and adds a touch of bohemian style to your garden.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?

Answer 2: When choosing the right size garden furniture, consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You should also think about the activities you plan to do in your garden, such as dining, lounging, or sunbathing.

Question 3: What are some tips for maintaining garden furniture?

Answer 3: To keep your garden furniture looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Clean your furniture regularly with a mild detergent and water.
  • Protect your furniture from the elements with a cover or sealant.
  • Store your furniture indoors or in a sheltered area during harsh weather conditions.
  • Tighten any screws or bolts regularly to ensure stability.
