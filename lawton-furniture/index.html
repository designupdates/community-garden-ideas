Tips

Here are a few tips for choosing and caring for garden furniture:

Tip 1: Choose the right materials. Garden furniture is exposed to the elements, so it is important to choose materials that are weather-resistant. Some of the most popular materials for garden furniture include teak, aluminum, and wicker.

Tip 2: Consider your needs. What will you be using your garden furniture for? If you plan on entertaining guests, you will need furniture that is comfortable and stylish. If you just need a place to relax, you can choose something more casual.

Tip 3: Measure your space. Before you buy any garden furniture, measure the space where you plan to put it. This will help you avoid buying furniture that is too big or too small.

Tip 4: Care for your furniture. To keep your garden furniture looking its best, clean it regularly with a damp cloth and mild soap solution. You should also store your furniture indoors during the winter months.
